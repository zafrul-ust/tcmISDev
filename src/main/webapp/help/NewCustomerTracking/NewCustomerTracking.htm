New Customer Tracking

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The New Customer Tracking page is used to view and approve requests to add new Distribution customers to tcmIS. The New Customer Tracking page can be accessed by selecting New Customer Tracking from the Distribution menu.

Figure 1

Click the Search button to see all new customer requests.

If you hover above some data cells, a tool tip will pop up and display the contents of the data cell. Typically tool tips will appear on columns where the data in the cell contains over 30 characters and the default column width does not display the entire contents of the cell. Use this feature to display the contents of cells without widening the column.

Figure 2

Select a status from the Status drop-down list to locate all new customer requests that are currently in the selected status. Click Search.

Figure 3

You may locate a request for a specific customer by selecting Customer from the Search drop-down box. Enter a portion of the customer's name in the Search text box. Click Search.

Figure 4

You may locate new customer requests submitted by a specific person by selecting Requestor from the Search drop-down box. Enter a portion of the requestor's name in the Search text box. Click Search.

Figure 5

You may locate new customer requests submitted for customers in a specific country by selecting Country from the Search drop-down box. Enter the country name in the Search text box. Click Search.

Figure 6

You may locate new customer requests by SAP Customer Code by selecting SAP Customer Code from the Search drop-down box. Enter the SAP Customer Code in the Search text box. Click Search.

Figure 7

You may also limit the results by request date. Click in the Request Date From and To boxes and select a date from the calendar. Click Search.

Figure 8

Click the Create Excel button to export the data on the New Customer Tracking page in Excel format.

You may also create a new customer request by clicking the New Customer button on the New Customer Tracking page. For more information on adding a new customer, see the Add Customer help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/CustomerAdd.htm.

If you are a new customer approver, you can view and approve or reject new customer requests by right-clicking anywhere on the line and selecting Show Details.

Figure 9

You may edit any information displayed on the Add Customer page. Click the Save link at the top left to save your changes. Click the Approve link to approve the new customer request. Click the Reject link to reject the new customer request.

For more information on the new customer request details, see the Add Customer help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/CustomerAdd.htm.

Figure 10

If the customer is rejected, you should enter a comment to explain why the new customer request was rejected. Click OK.

Figure 11

To locate new customer requests that were saved as drafts, select Draft from the Status drop-down box. Click Search. To return to the request, right-click anywhere on the line and select Show Details.

Figure 12

Complete the request as described in the Add Customer help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/CustomerAdd.htm.

Click the Submit link at the top left of the page to submit the new customer request.

Figure 13

Click the Delete link at the top left of the Add Customer page to delete draft new customer requests. Draft new customer requests can only be deleted by the original requestor.
