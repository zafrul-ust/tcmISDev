The Edit Customer process is used edit customer information for tcmIS Distribution Hubs. You may begin the Edit Customer process from the Customer Search page. For more information on the Customer Search page, see the Customer Search help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/CustomerSearch.htm.

To update existing customer information, locate the customer and right-click anywhere on the line. Select Show Details.

Figure 1

The Customer Details page is displayed. If you have the necessary permissions, you may edit any of the fields in the Customer Details section of the Customer Details page.

Update Permanent Bill To Notes in the Notes section. Click the Update link to save changes.

Figure 2

Click the Tax Exemption Certificate link to view or add the customer's tax exemption certification number. Click Add and select the country and state or province from the corresponding drop-down lists. Enter the tax exemption certificate number in the corresponding column. Some certificates may have expiration dates. If so, enter the expiration date by clicking in the Expire Date text box and selecting a date from the calendar. Repeat this process to add more than one Tax Exemption Certificate. Click the Process link to save changes. Click a line and click the Remove link to delete a Tax Exemption Certificate.

Figure 3

You may open and close the various sections of the Customer Details page by clicking the and icons respectively.

You may add a new Customer Contact in the Contact Info section of the Customer Details page by clicking the New Contact link.

Figure 4

To edit an existing customer contact right-click anywhere on the contact and select Edit Contact.

Figure 5

The customer contact information is displayed. Edit or enter the necessary information and click the OK button. You may designate the contact as the Default Contact for this customer by checking the box labeled Default Contact. The default contact will be automatically populated as the contact on Scratch PAds, Quotes, and Material Requests. There can be only one default contact per customer. So if another contact is marked as default, making any other contact the default contact will overwrite the current default contact.

Figure 6

You may inactivate a customer contact by unchecking the box in the Status column and clicking the Update link in the Customer Details section.

You may also change the default contact for a customer by checking the box in the Default Contact column. Click the Update link in the Customer Details section to save any changes.

Figure 7

The contact is now inactive and displayed in black.

To re-activate the contact, check the box in the Status column and clicking the Update link in the Customer Details section.

Figure 8

You may add a new Ship To Location for the customer by clicking the New Ship To link in the Ship To section of the Customer Details page. Click the Update link in the Customer Details section to save the new location.

You may change the price list associated with a Ship To address by selecting the price list from the corresponding drop-down list in the Ship To section. Click the Update link in the Customer Details section to save to save changes.

The Sales Agent and Field Sales Agent can be updated for each Ship To as well. Click the in the appropriate column and look up and enter the agent's name. Contact the Technical Center to add a new agent. Click the Update link in the Customer Details section to save changes.

You may update notes associated with a Ship To address by clicking in the Ship To Notes column and editing or adding notes. Click the Update link in the Customer Details section to save changes.

You may not edit the Ship To Address. You may mark an address as inactive by unchecking the box in the Active column and create a new Ship To Address.

Figure 9

You may add a new freight carrier for the customer by clicking the New Carrier link in the Ship To section of the Customer Details page.

Figure 10

Select the carrier from the Carrier Name drop-down list. If the carrier does not exist in the list click the New button. Enter the customer's account number for the selected carrier in the Carrier Account box. You may enter any notes regarding this carrier account in the Notes box. Click OK to add the carrier and account to the selected customer.

Figure 11

Click the Update link in the Customer Details section to save the new carrier.

Click the button labeled Credit Review to view credit information and details including Outstanding Accounts Receivable, Available Credit, Past Due Aging, Unapplied Cash, Open Invoices and Open Orders.
