Customer Lookup

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Customer Lookup function is used to locate current distribution customers and ship to locations from a Scratch Pad. A customer must be selected on a Scratch Pad to begin the Quoting or Material Request entry process.

You may begin the Customer Lookup process from the Scratch Pad by clicking the labeled Customer.

Figure 1

You may search for a customer using the Customer Name by selecting contains from the Customer Name drop-down list and entering a portion of the name in the Customer Name text box. Click Search.

If you hover above some data cells, a tool tip will pop up and display the contents of the data cell. Typically tool tips will appear on columns where the data in the cell contains over 30 characters and the default column width does not display the entire contents of the cell. Use this feature to display the contents of cells without widening the column.

Figure 2

If you know the Customer ID, you may locate the customer be entering it in the Customer ID text box. Click Search.

Figure 3

You may also locate a customer using the Bill To description. select contains from the Bill To drop-down list and enter a portion of the Bill To description in the Bill To text box. Click Search.

Figure 4

You may also locate a customer using the Ship To location. Select contains from the Ship To drop-down list and enter a portion of the Ship To description in the Ship To text box. Click Search.

Figure 5

You may also locate a customer using the legacy Customer ID. Select contains from the Synonym drop-down list and enter a portion of the legacy Customer ID in the Synonym text box. Click Search.

Figure 6

To select a customer and add it to the Scratch Pad simply click the desired Ship To location and then click the Return Selected Customer link above the data grid. The customer will be populated on the Scratch Pad.

Figure 7

Click the Create Excel Button to export the data in the results grid to Excel format.

Click the New Customer button to create a new customer. For more information on adding a new customer, see the Add Customer help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/CustomerAdd.htm.
