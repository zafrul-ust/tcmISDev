Add Customer

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Add Customer process is used to request the addition of new customers to tcmIS for Distribution Hubs. You may begin the Add Customer process from the Scratch Pad by clicking the labeled Customer. First search to see if the customer already exists. For more information on the Customer Lookup process see the Customer Lookup help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/CustomerLookup.htm.

If a new customer needs to be added, click the New Customer button.

Figure 1

The Add Customer page is displayed. All fields marked with an asterisk are required fields and must be completed.

Figure 2

A customer is defined as a distinct Bill To Address. A company could have several Bill To Addresses. The first step in adding a new customer is to look up the company to see if there are already other existing Bill To Addresses in tcmIS. Click the labeled Company.

Enter the company name and click Search. If the company is found select the company by clicking anywhere on the line. Then click the Return Selected Data link above the data grid.

Figure 3

The Company field will be populated on the Add Customer page.

Figure 4

If the company is not found on the Company Search page, close the page. Check the box labeled New on the Add Customer page. Enter the company name, a short name for the company, and the legal company name in the corresponding text boxes. If you are unsure of this information, you may leave it blank and it will be assigned at a later date.

Enter the Customer Name in the corresponding text box.

Enter the Bill To Address in the Full Bill To Address text boxes. These are free form fields and you may enter the address in any format as long as the City and Postal Code are included.

In the Address Indexing are, select the country from the Country drop-down box. If required, select the state or region from the State/Region drop-down box. Enter the City and Postal Code in the corresponding text boxes.

In the Business Info section, enter applicable tax registration information. If the customer is in the US, a Federal Tax ID or Social Security Number must be provided. If the customer is in Canada, the GST and PST registration numbers must be provided. If the supplier is outside the US or Canada, a VAT registration number must be provided. Select the appropriate tax registration type from the Registration drop-down list and enter the corresponding registration number in the Number text box. Up to four different registration numbers can be entered.

Select the customer's industry, consolidation method, payment terms, and price list from the corresponding drop-down lists.

Specify the customer's default shelf life in the text box labeled Default Shelf Life.

Check the box Charge Freight if the customer should be charged for freight. If the customer provides their freight account then do not check this box. If the customer does not provide a freight account, and needs to be invoiced for freight, check this box.

Select the ship complete rule from the Ship Complete drops down list. Select No if partial shipments are acceptable for this customer. Select Line if each line of the order should ship complete. Select Order if all lines on the order should ship complete.

Select Y or N whether the customer would like to receive order acknowledgements by email.

Check the box labeled Mfg CoC if the customer requires a Certificate of Conformance delivered with all orders. Check the box labeled Mfg CoA if the customer requires a Certificate of Analysis delivered with all orders. Check the box labeled MSDS if the customer requires a Material Safety Data Sheet delivered with all orders.

Select the currency to invoice taxes in from the Tax Currency drop-down box.

Enter the customer's credit limit in the Credit Limit text box. The credit limit should be entered in the same currency as the material currency. Enter the overdue limit in the corresponding text box. The overdue limit can be entered as a number of days or as a percent. Select the desired method from drop-down list.

Figure 5

Specify the field sales representative by clicking the labeled Field Sales Rep. Enter the name of the field sales representative in the Search box and click Search. Select the desired name by clicking anywhere on the line. Then click the link labeled Return Selected User. The name will be populated in the Field Sales Rep field on the Customer Add page.

Figure 6

Scroll down to the Contact info section. Customer contacts should be entered in this section by clicking the Add Line link and entering the necessary contact information. Be sure to scroll to the right to enter all the required fields. Required fields are marked with an asterisk.

Customer Ship To Addresses should be entered in the Ship To section by clicking Add Line and entering the necessary information. Be sure to scroll to the right to enter all the required fields. Required fields are marked with an asterisk.

You may also add customer freight accounts in the Carrier section in the same manner. Click Add Line and specify the carrier name by clicking the in the Carrier Name column.

Figure 7

Select the freight carrier from the Carrier Name drop-down box. Enter the customer's account number in the Carrier Account text box. Enter any important notes in the Notes text box. Click the OK button.

If the freight carrier does not appear in the Carrier Name drop-down list, click New.

Figure 8

Enter the carrier name in the corresponding text box. Select the transportation mode and carrier method from the corresponding drop-down lists. Click OK. You may now select the new carrier from the Carrier Name drop-down list.

Figure 9

For customers in the US or Canada, you may enter tax exemption certificate numbers in the section called Tax Exemption Certificate. Click Add Line and select the country and state or province from the corresponding drop-down lists. Enter the tax exemption certificate number in the corresponding column. Some certificates may have expiration dates. If so, enter the expiration date by clicking in the Expire Date text box and selecting a date from the calendar.

Include any other important information in the Notes text box.

Figure 10

After all required fields have been completed, submit the request by clicking the Submit link at the top of the page. You may also save or delete the request by clicking the corresponding links at the top of the page.

Figure 11

All requests will be sent for approval. The requestor will be notified when the customer has been added. The status of the customer add requests can be viewed from the New Customer Tracking page. For more information on tracking and approving new customer requests, see the New Customer Tracking help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerTracking/CustomerTracking.htm.

To update existing customer information, you should access the Customer Search page from the Distribution menu. For more information on viewing and updating existing customer information, see the Customer Search help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/CustomerSearch.htm and the Edit Customer help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/EditCustomer.htm.
