Picking QC

+ Larger Font | - Smaller Font

Picking QC is the process of independently checking materials picked for delivery before final packaging and shipment to the customer. The completed pick list is compared against material staged for delivery to confirm that the correct receipt ID and quantity has been picked and that the materials are properly labeled.

Watch the following movie to learn more about the Picking QC process: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/PickingQC/PickingQCMovie.htm.

Picking QC is completed in tcmIS on the Picking QC page.This page can be accessed from the Supply Chain Menu. Select Stock Room Operations. Under Delivery, choose Picking QC.

Figure 1

Select the Hub for which picking will be performed from the Hub drop-down box. Click Search to display all Material Requests which are ready for Picking QC.

Figure 2

To display all Material Requests which are ready for Picking QC for a specific facility, select the desired facility from the Facility drop-down box and click Search.

Figure 3

To locate a specific Material Request which is ready for Picking QC, enter the MR number in the MR text box and click Search.

Figure 4

To locate materials which are ready for picking from a recent pick list, select the pick list ID from the Pick List drop-down box and click Search.

Figure 5

Enter the quantity picked in the Qty Picked column on the far right of the Picking QC page. Check the Print box in the far left column of the Picking QC page. Click Confirm.

If the quantity picked for an individual receipt ID is different from the suggested pick quantity on the pick list, tcmIS will accept the new quantity and adjust the inventory accordingly.

If the total quantity picked on the Picking QC page is less than the total suggested pick quantity on the pick list, the remaining materials to be picked will appear on a new pick list after the current pick list has completed the Ship Confirmation process.

If the total quantity picked on the Picking QC page is greater than the total suggested pick quantity on the pick list, the quantity on the Material Request must be increased. Contact the Technical Center for assistance.

Bill of Ladings can be printed from the Picking QC page by clicking Print BOL Long or Print BOL Short.

Box labels can be printed by clicking Print BOX labels. Select the label size and complete the Deliver To, Part No., and No. of Labels fields. Click Generate Labels.

Figure 6

Labels can be reprinted from the Picking QC page by chcecking the Print box on the far left and then clicking Re-Print Labels. Choose the label size and enter the storage temperature, part number(s), expiration date, recerts, and the number of labels to print. Some of this information may already be completed and simply needs to be verified. Click Generate Labels to reprint labels.

Figure 7

To print a written record of the delivery, click Delivery Ticket.

Figure 8

If you need to delete the record and begin a new pick, click the link in the MR Line column and select Yes when prompted to reverse the MR Line. Return the the Picking page to pick the material again.

Figure 9

To view and edit an item's DOT shipping name and other shipping information, click the link in the Item column. The DOT Shipping Name page is displayed.

Figure 10

The proper shipping name can be chosen by clicking Lookup.

Figure 11

Select the proper shipping name and click OK to return to the DOT Shipping Name page. Click Ship Name to display the chosen shipping name as the Final Shipping Name and click Update.

The Ship Confirmation process documents the delivery of materials to the customer. After completion of Picking QC, proceed to the Ship Confirm Page.
