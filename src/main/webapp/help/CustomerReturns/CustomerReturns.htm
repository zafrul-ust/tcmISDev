Customer Returns

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Customer Returns page is used to receive material returned from a customer into tcmIS. Please note that currently, materials returned from the Customer Returns page will not result in a credit to the customer. Material will be received in as customer owned. To process a return from credit, contact the Technical Center. The Customer Returns page can be accessed from the Supply Chain menu by selecting Stock Room Operations. Under Delivery, select Customer Returns. The page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select the hub from which the material was originally delivered from the Hub drop-down list. You may also select the facility that is returning the material from the Facility drops-down box.

Enter the receipt id that was returned in the Receipt ID text box. The receipt ID can found on the Haas TCM label on the material under the barcode. Click Search.

Figure 2

Alternatively, you can enter the Item ID in the Item text box and click Search to locate all deliveries of an item to the selected facility.

Figure 3

You may enter the customer part number in the Part text box and click Search to locate all deliveries of a customer part number to the selected facility.

Figure 4

You may also enter the material request number in the MR text box and click Search to locate all deliveries on the specified material request to the selected facility.

Figure 5

Select the bin location where the material is to be stored from the Bin drop-down list. If the desired bin does not appear in the list, click the + sign to the left of the Bin drop-down list to select from all hub bins.

Select the bin from the drop-down list and click OK.

Figure 6

Enter the date the material was received at the hub in the DOR column. Enter the quantity of each Receipt ID that was returned in the Qty Returned column. Check the box in the OK column for all lines to receive into inventory. ClickReceive.

Figure 7

The material will now appear on the Receiving QC page. Perform receiving QC and print labels as usual.
