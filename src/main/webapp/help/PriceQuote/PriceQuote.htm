Price Quote

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Price Quote page displays price quote requests and facilitates entering supplier quotations in tcmIS. The Price Quote page can be accessed from the Supply Chain menu by selecting Purchasing. Select Price Quote and then select Price Quote Page. The Price Quote page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select a name from the drop-down box labeled Show items for assigned quoter to see all price quote requests assigned to a specific person. Click the Resubmit & Sort button to display the price quote requests.

Figure 2

Enter a customer part number in the box labeled where the Client Part # contains to see all price quote requests for the customer part number. Click the Resubmit & Sort button to display the price quote requests.

Figure 3

Enter an Item ID in the box labeled where the Haas Item ID is to see all price quote requests for the Item ID. Click the Resubmit & Sort button to display the price quote requests.

Figure 4

Enter an manufacturer name in the box labeled where the Manufacturer contains to see all price quote requests for items produced by the specified manufacturer. Click the Resubmit & Sort button to display the price quote requests.

Figure 5

Select a hub from the drop-down box labeled where the Branch Plant (Hub) is to see all price quote requests for a specific hub. Click the Resubmit & Sort button to display the price quote requests.

Figure 6

You may sort by Priority, Haas Item ID, Item Description, or Client Part Number/Hub/Catalog by selecting the sort option from the Sort by drop-down list. Click the Resubmit & Sort button to sort the price quote requests.

Click the link in the Item ID column to view buyer item notes for that item. Enter notes in the text box and click Update. Click Delete to delete the item note.

Figure 7

You may assign a price quote request to someone by selecting their name from the Quoter drop-down list and clicking Assign Quoters.

Figure 8

Click the New link in the Quote 1 column to enter a supplier price quote. Enter the price, lead time and supplier in the corresponding text boxes. Enter any additional charges or minimum order quantities in the appropriate text boxes in the Additional Charge area of the Price Quote page. Click Enter Quote to save the supplier quotation.

Figure 9

The Quote 1 column is now populated with the price entered. The first Supplier column displays the supplier entered. Click Edit to edit the quotation.

Repeat the process for Quote 2 and Quote 3.

Figure 10

If it is not possible to obtain more quotes, check the box labeled This is the last quote I can possibly get because. Enter a reason in the text box. The price quote request is completed when either three quotes is entered or this box is checked on a price quote.
