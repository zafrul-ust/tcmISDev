Dropship Receiving

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Dropship Receiving page is used by Haas TCM customers or staff to record the receipt of an order in tcmIS when the material was shipped directly to the customer's facility and did not pass through a Haas TCM hub. The dropship receiving process essentially creates a receipt into inventory and a delivery against a Material Request in tcmIS all in one step. The Dropship Receiving page can be accessed from the Order Management menu by selecting Dropship Receiving. The page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select the Operating Entity and Hub for which dropship receiving will be performed from the corresponding drop-down boxes. Select an Inventory Group from the Inventory Group drop-down list to see all open dropship orders for the selected Inventory Group. Select My Inventory Groups to see all open dropship orders for Inventory Groups at the selected Hub for which you have access. Click Search to display all open dropship orders which are ready to be received. By default only orders expected to be delivered in the next 30 days are listed. To see more orders, increase the number of days in the Expected Within box.

Figure 2

You may also select a Ship To location from the Ship To drop-down box to display open dropship orders for a specific location. Click the Search button to display open dropship orders.

Figure 3

You may search for orders for a particular Item ID by selecting Item ID from the Search by drop-down list and entering the Item ID in the text box. Click Search to see all open dropship orders for the Item ID entered.

Figure 4

You may search for orders for a particular Material Request by selecting MR Number from the Search by drop-down list and entering the Material Request number in the text box. Click Search to see all open dropship orders for the material request number entered.

Figure 5

You may search for orders for a particular Haas TCM Purchase Order by selecting Haas PO from the Search by drop-down list and entering the PO number in the text box. Click Search to see all open dropship orders for the PO number entered.

Figure 6

You may view the Purchase Order by right-clicking on the line and selecting View Purchase Order. The PO is opened in a new window.

Figure 7

You may also search for orders for a particular material using the Item Description. Select Item Desc and contains from the Search by drop-down lists and enter a portion of the Item Description in the text box. Click Search to see all open dropship orders matching the Item Description entered.

Figure 8

To complete dropship receiving in tcmIS, begin by entering the manufacturer's lot number from the material in the Lot column.

If the supplier ship date is known, enter the date in the Act Supp Ship column by clicking in the text box and selecting the date from the calendar. This is not a required field and can be left blank if this information is not provided by the supplier.

Enter the actual date the material was delivered at the customer facility in the DOR column by clicking in the text box and selecting the date from the calendar.

Enter the expiration date of the material in the Exp Date column by clicking in the text box and selecting the date from the calendar. If this is not a shelf life sensitive product, click the Indefinite button at the bottom of the calendar.

Figure 9

Enter the quantity delivered in the QTY Rec'd column. Note that it is very important to receive material in terms of the unit of measure displayed in the packaging field.

Enter any notes in the Notes text box.

Figure 10

If the material delivered has more than one lot number or expiration date, click the Dupl button to split the shipment into multiple lines and complete all required fields for both lines.

Figure 11

Some items are not always filled the same amount for each delivery. These items are known as variably sized or MV items. For MV items on the Dropship Receiving page, you must enter the quantity and size in the Packaged Qty x Packaged Size column. If more than one size was delivered, click the Dup Pkg button and enter the Packaged Qty x Packaged Size for each container.

Figure 12

After all required data fields have been entered, check the box in the OK column for all lines to receive. Click the Receive link above the data grid.

Figure 13

A summary of all receipts made is displayed. This page can be printed for your records by clicking Print This Page. If the customer is equipped with a Zebra printer, and it has been configured to work with tcmIS, container labels can be printed by clicking Container Labels. Contact the Technical Center to enable label printing.

Figure 14

Click the Create Excel button on the Dropship Receiving page to export a list of open orders to Excel.
