Dropship Receiving

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Dropship Receiving page is used by Haas TCM customers to record the receipt of an order in tcmIS when the material was shipped directly to the customer's facility and did not pass through a Haas TCM hub. The Dropship Receiving page can be accessed from the Order Management menu by selecting Dropship Receiving. The page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select the hub, inventory group, and ship to location from the corresponding drop-down lists. Click Search to see all open dropship orders. By default only orders expected to be delivered in the next 30 days are listed. To see more orders, increase the number of days in the Expected Within box.

Figure 2

You may search for orders for a particular Item ID by selecting Item ID from the Search by drop-down list and entering the Item ID in the text box. Click Search to see all open dropship orders for the Item ID entered.

Figure 3

You may search for orders for a particular material request by selecting MR Number from the Search by drop-down list and entering the material request number in the text box. Click Search to see all open dropship orders for the material request number entered.

Figure 4

You may search for orders for a particular Haas TCM purchase order by selecting Haas PO from the Search by drop-down list and entering the PO number in the text box. Click Search to see all open dropship orders for the PO number entered.

Figure 5

To complete dropship receiving in tcmIS, begin by entering the manufacturer's lot number from the material in the Lot column.

If the supplier ship date is known, enter the date in the Act Supp Ship column. Click the blue diamond to select the date from the calendar. This is not a required field and can be left blank if this information is not provided by the supplier.

Enter the actual date the material was delivered at the customer facility in the DOR column. Click the blue diamond to select the date from the calendar.

Enter the expiration date of the material in the Exp Date column. Click the blue diamond to select the date from the calendar. If this is not a shelf life sensitive product, enter the word Indefinite.

Enter the quantity delivered in the QTY Rec'd column. Note that it is very important to receive material in terms of the unit of measure displayed in the packaging field.

Enter any notes in the Notes text box.

Figure 6

If the material delivered has more than one lot number or expiration date, click the Dup Line button to split the shipment into multiple lines and complete all required fields for both lines.

Figure 7

Some items are not always filled the same amount for each delivery. These items are known as variably sized or MV items. For MV items on the Dropship Receiving page, you must enter the quantity and size in the Packaged Qty x Packaged Size column. If more than one size was delivered, click the Dup Pkg button and enter the Packaged Qty x Packaged Size for each container.

Figure 8

After all required data fields have been entered, check the box in the OK column for all lines to receive. Click the Receive link above the data grid.

Figure 9

A summary of all receipts made is displayed. This page can be printed for your records by clicking Print This Page. If the customer is equipped with a Zebra printer, and it has been configured to work with tcmIS, container labels can be printed by clicking Container Labels. Contact the Technical Center to enable label printing.
