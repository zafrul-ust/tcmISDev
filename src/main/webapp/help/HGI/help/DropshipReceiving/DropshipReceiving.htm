Dropship Receiving

The Dropship Receiving page is used by HGI customers or staff to record the receipt of an order in tcmIS when the material was shipped directly to the customer's facility and did not pass through an HGI hub. The dropship receiving process essentially creates a receipt into inventory and a delivery against a Material Request in tcmIS all in one step. The Dropship Receiving page can be accessed from the Order Management menu by selecting Dropship Receiving. The page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

There are a number of selections that define the search:

  • You must use the Operating Entity and Hub drop-down lists to select those for which dropship receiving will be performed. In some cases there is only a single Inventory Group associated with the Hub. In other cases the Inventory Group drop-down list allows you to choose either the default My Inventory Groups or select one particular Inventory Group. If you do select a particular Inventory Group, there may be a further choice available in the Ship To drop-down list of the default All Docks or you may choose one particular dock.
  • You may provide something to at least partially identify the material you want using the Search by boxes. Your search may be specified by Item Desc[ription], Item ID, MR Number, or Haas PO. See "below and Search functions for more information.
  • You may specify the order in which the items are listed using the Sort list.
    • by date expected and, within date, by supplier when you select Date Exptd/Supplier;
    • by Material Request line number when you select MR-Line;
    • by Purchase Order and, within PO, by line when you select PO/PO Line;
    • by supplier and, within supplier, by date expected when you select Supplier/Date Exptd.
  • By default only orders expected to be delivered in the next 30 days are listed. To see more orders, increase the number of days in the Expected Within box.
  • To limit the dropship receiving activity to items for a particular customer, click the magnifying glass icon across from the Customer label to open the Customer Lookup form. See Lookup forms for further information.

Click the Search button.

Click the Create Excel button on the page to export the data in Excel format.

Figure 2

The customer selection form provides five different selections to specify exactly which customer is to be used for the dropship receiving activity. You may use any or all of them.

  • The Customer ID search term always uses the is drop-down item to locate an exact match to any customer ID number entered.
  • The remaining search terms,
    • Customer Name
    • Synonym
    • Bill To
    • Ship To
  • allow you to choose the types of match: is; contains; starts with; or ends with. See Search functions for more information.
  • Do not use any combination that results in excluding the customer you want. For example, there may be no customer with a Bill To containing 'Florida' and a Ship To containing 'Kenya'.

Click the Search button, select the row with the customer you want, and click the Return Selected Customer link that will appear at the top of the list. The Customer will be added to the Dropship Receiving search form and included in the search criteria for that page.

Figure 4

When you have located the shipment you want to 'receive' (by entering details into tcmIS), use the input boxes on the selected line of the Dropship Receiving page to enter the needed details. Scroll the page to the left to view the columns if necessary.

  • Although the OK check box is the first field available for action, it is actually the very last thing to do when receiving the order into tcmIS. Fill in all the other information before you select this box.
  • Begin by entering the manufacturer's lot number from the material in the Lot column.
  • If the supplier ship date is known, enter that date in the Actual Supplier Ship Date column. Place your cursor in the field and the Date Selector form will open to allow you to choose the date. This is not a required field and can be left blank if this information is not provided by the supplier.
  • Enter the actual date the material was delivered to your facility in the DOR ("date of receipt") column. The date defaults to today's date which you may leave if that is correct. Click your cursor in the field and the Date Selector form will open to allow you to choose another date (but you may not use a date later than today).
  • Enter the expiration date of the material in the Exp Date column. Place your cursor in the field and the Date Selector form will open to allow you to choose the date. If this is not a shelf life sensitive product, enter the word Indefinite by clicking the Indefinite button at the bottom of the calendar form.
  • Enter the quantity delivered in the Qty Rec'd column. Note that it is very important to receive material in terms of the unit of measure displayed in the packaging field.
  • Enter any notes in the Notes text box.
  • Provide the supplier's delivery ticket information in the Delivery Ticket text box.
  • Select the country of origin for the material from the Ship From Country list.
Figure 5

If the material delivered has more than a single lot number or more than a single expiration date, split the shipment into enough lines to record each lot number and expiration date combination on a separate line. Click the Dupl[icate] button to add a split line, repeating until you have all the lines you need for the shipment. Complete all the required fields for each line.

Figure 6

Items are sometimes shipped in different quantities in different deliveries. These items are known as variably sized or MV items. For MV items on the Dropship Receiving page, you must enter both quantity and size in the Packaged Qty x Packaged Size column. If more than one size was delivered, use the Dup Kit/Pkg button as needed and enter the Packaged Qty x Packaged Size for each size.

Figure 7

Complete all the data entry for each line received. After all lines are complete, select the check box in the OK column for each completed line.

After all lines are marked OK, click the Receive link at the top left of the data grid to complete the process. Unchecked lines will be left for future action.
