Catalog Addition Requests - HMRF tab

The Cat Add Req (Catalog Addition Request) page with its HMRF tab is used to view and manage Hazardous Material Review Form information for the products on the QPL. It is accessed after opening the Cat Add Req page (from the Catalog page) by clicking on the HMRF tab.

As you can see there are five individual tabs in the lower portion of the Cat Add Req page which display different sets of information. The tab in use (active) at any time is shown with white lettering on a dark background; the inactive tabs are shown with dark lettering on a light background.

  • QPL - used to view and manage products on or being requested for the QPL.
  • Use Approval - used to view and manage use permissions on products on the QPL.
  • Spec - used to view and manage specifications for the products on the QPL.
  • Flowdown - used to view and manage flowdowns for the products on the QPL.
  • HMRF - used to view and manage Hazardous Material Review Form information for the products on the QPL.

Figure 1

Record the Hazardous Material Review Form information on this tab.

  • Select the intended purpose of the material from the Used As drop-down list.
  • If the material is to, or may, leave the USA, check the To Be Sent Outside U.S. box.
  • Indicate the type of use for the material from the Type of Use drop-down list.
  • Enter the Type of Material in that text box.
  • If the type of material is an adhesive, check either the Autoclavable or Structural box, as appropriate.
  • Identify whether the material is used with flying or non-flying parts by selecting from the Applied to drop-down list.

When all the HMRF information is added, click the Save link in the upper-left of the HMRF section to store the information with the current Modify QPL request.
