Ship Confirmation

+ Larger Font | - Smaller Font

The Ship Confirmation process documents the delivery of materials to the customer. The delivery date recorded in tcmIS is the date the materials leave the Haas facility, not the date the material arrives at the customer facility. This date is used to determine the on time delivery rate of materials to the customer. TcmIS uses the current date as the default date unless an alternate date is provided.

Ship Confirmation is completed in tcmIS on the Ship Confirm page. This page can be accessed from the Supply Chain menu. Select Stock Room Operations, Under Delivery, choose Ship Confirm.

Figure 1

Select the Operating Entity from the corresponding drop-down list. Select the Hub and Inventory Group from the corresponding drop-down lists. You may select My Inventory Groups to see all Material Requests ready to be Ship Confirmed at the selected hub for all Inventory Groups for which you have access. Click Search to display all Material Requests which are ready to be Ship Confirmed.

Figure 2

You may display all Material Requests ready to be shipped for a specific customer by clicking the to the right of the Customer field. For more details on the Customer Lookup process, see the Customer Lookup help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/CustomerLookup2.htm. Click Search to display all completed picks for the selected customer.

Click the Clear button to remove the customer name from the Customer field.

Figure 3
For Distribution, you may select a CSR from the corresponding drop-down list to see all MRs created by the selected CSR. Click Search to see all MRs which are ready for shipment.

Figure 4

To view a specific Material Request, enter the MR Number in the Search text box and click the Search button.

Figure 5

There are two possible Ship Confirm processes. Some hubs must create shipments prior to completing the ship confirmation process. Other hubs simply confirm each MR Line as its own shipment.

If the Shipment ID column is not displayed on the Ship Confirm page, simply select the MR Lines to confirm by checking the box in the OK column. Enter the date the material shipped by clicking in the Date Delivered box and selecting a date from the calendar. Today's date is populated by default, but it should be changed if the material shipped on a different date.

Click the Print BOL Long or Print BOL Short link to print Bill of Ladings for the Material Requests. Click the Delivery Labels link to print box labels.

Click the Confirm Shipment button.

Figure 6

If the Shipment ID column is displayed on the Ship Confirm page, then the first step in the Ship Confirmation process is to create shipments. Materials going to the same Ship To location may be grouped on the same shipment if they will be delivered on the same Bill of Lading. Group all MRs that will ship together by checking the OK box for all MR lines to include in the shipment. Click the Create Shipment link.

Figure 7

The Shipment ID is populated by tcmIS. You may change the Shipment ID by selecting a different shipment from the Shipment ID drop-down box and clicking Update Shipment.

You may remove a MR Line from a shipment by selecting the blank selection from the Shipment ID drop-down box. The OK box is checked automatically. Click the Update Shipment link to remove the MR Line from the shipment.

Figure 8

Click the Delivery Labels link to print shipping labels. Select the label size and complete the Deliver To, Part No., and No. of Labels fields. Click Generate Labels.

Figure 9

Bill of Ladings can be printed from the Ship Confirm page. Select the shipments to print BOLs for by checking the OK box on the left of each line. Click Print BOL Long, Print BOL Short or Consolidated BOL.

For Distribution Inventory Groups you may print a Packing List, Certifications, or MSDS for a shipment by selecting Shipment from the drop-down box labeled Documents for and choosing the Shipment ID. You may also select MR and choose the MR number from the drop-down box to print documents associated with a specific MR. Click the Print link.

Figure 10

Check the corresponding check box to print a Packing List, Certification, and/or MSDS. Click the Print button.

Figure 11

For Distribution Inventory Groups, header charges or line charges can be added to the MR from the Ship Confirm page by right-clicking on a line and selecting Header Charges or Line Charges. For more information on adding charges to a Material Request see the Additional Charges help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/OrderEntry/AdditionalCharges.htm.

You may view the Material Request being shipped by right-clicking on the line and selecting Open MR.

Figure 12

Finally, after all picks to be shipped have been assigned Shipment IDs, click the Confirm Shipment link.

Figure 13

If the ship confirmation date is different from the current date, enter that date in the Date Delivered box by clicking in the box and selecting the date from the calendar.

Select the freight carrier from the Carrier drop-down list. Enter the Tracking Number.

Select the shipments to be Ship Confirmed by checking the OK box on the left. Click Confirm Shipment.

Figure 14

For Distribution Inventory Groups, you may print the Invoice associated with the shipment after it has been confirmed by clicking the Print Invoice link.

Figure 15

After completion of the Ship Confirm process, material is considered delivered in tcmIS. Inventory is decremented when the Ship Confirm process is completed.

Ship Confirmation of a quantity of zero will put the Material Request back on the Picking page.
