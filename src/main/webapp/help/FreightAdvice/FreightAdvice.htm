Freight Advice

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Freight Advice page is used to lookup and change shipping instructions primarily for deliveries to the U.S. Government. Freight advice is transmitted to Haas TCM from a Logistics company and displayed on the Freight Advice page. Haas TCM warehouse employees can alter the freight advice if needed. The Freight Advice page can be accessed from the Supply Chain menu by selecting Stock Room Operations. Under Delivery, select Freight Advice. The page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select the hub and inventory group from the corresponding drop-down lists.

Enter a date in the Projected Ship Date field by clicking in the text box and selecting a date from the calendar.

You may select a shipment status from the Ship Status drop-down list or select All to list all freight advice.

Click Search to display shipments and the associated freight advice.

Figure 2

You may select Packing Group ID from the Search box and enter the packing group id in the text box to the right of the Search box. Click the Search button to display all freight advice for the packing group.

Figure 3

You may select Carrier from the Search box and enter the carrier name in the text box to the right of the Search box. Click the Search button to display all freight advice for the carrier.

Figure 4

You may select Tracking Number from the Search box and enter the tracking number in the text box to the right of the Search box. Click the Search button to display all freight advice for the tracking number.

Figure 5

You may select Consolidation No from the Search box and enter the consolidation number in the text box to the right of the Search box. Click the Search button to display all freight advice for the consolidation number.

Figure 6

You may select MILSTRIP from the Search box and enter the MILSTRIP number in the text box to the right of the Search box. Click the Search button to display all freight advice for the MILSTRIP number.

Figure 7

You may select MR from the Search box and enter the material request ID in the text box to the right of the Search box. Click the Search button to display all freight advice for the material request.

Figure 8

Right-click on any line and select Change Freight Advice to edit the freight advice for the selected line.

Figure 9

Select LTL or Parcel from the Transportation Mode drop-down list.

You may change the projected ship date by clicking the blue diamond and selecting a date from the calendar.

Type notes to document why the change was made. Click OK to update the freight advice.

Figure 10

If you hover above some data cells, a tool tip will pop up and display the contents of the data cell. Typically tool tips will appear on columns where the data in the cell contains over 30 characters and the default column width does not display the entire contents of the cell. Use this feature to display the contents of cells without widening the column.

Figure 11

You may sort by any column by clicking the column header. Click the column header again to sort in descending order.

Figure 12

Click Create Excel File on the Freight Advice page to export the report into Excel.
