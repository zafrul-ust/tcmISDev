Low Stock

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Low Stock page provides information regarding potential stock outs based on historical consumption and current inventory. The Low Stock page can be accessed from the Reports menu by selecting Inventory and then Low Stock. The Low Stock page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select the Operating Entity from the corresponding drop-down list. Select the Hub and Inventory Group from the corresponding drops-down lists. You may select My Hubs or My Inventory Groups to see all hubs and/or inventory groups for which you have permission.

Enter a number of days in the Less than ___ Days of Inventory box to see all products where on hand inventory is less than historical usage for that time period. Click Search.

For each Item. the quantity in inventory and average cost is displayed. The number of days until stock out based on usage for various time periods is also displayed.

Figure 2

Right-click on an individual line and select Show Inventory Detail to see more information regarding the inventory.

Figure 3

Details regarding the Receipt ID including lot status, manufacturer lot number, expiration date, and warehouse bin location are displayed.

Figure 4

Click the Create Excel File button on the Low Stock page to export the results into Excel format.
