Transfer Request

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Transfer Request page is to facilitate inventory transfers between hubs in tcmIS. This page can be accessed from the Supply Chain Menu by selecting Transfer Request. The Transfer Request page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select the Hub and Inventory Group from which the material is to be transferred. Search for the material to be transferred by entering the Item ID. You may also search for the material by Item Description. Click Search to locate the material. The quantity available is displayed. Enter the Quantity being transferred in the text box labeled Quantity to Send. Click Process to submit the transfer request.

Figure 2

The material to be transferred will be displayed on the Picking page for the hub that is shipping the product. Perform Picking, Picking QC as usual.

Once the pick is confirmed on the Currently CMS transfers will not allocate if inventory on hand is less than the min stocking level.
Once the pick is confirmed on the Packing QC page, it will appear on the Receiving page of the hub receiving the material. Perform Receiving and Receiving QC as usual. This actions results in Ship Confirmation at the original hub.

The material will now be in inventory at the hub it was shipped to and issued from inventory from the original hub.
