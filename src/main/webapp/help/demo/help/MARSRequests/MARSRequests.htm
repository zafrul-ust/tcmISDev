MARS Requests

All MARS Requests (Incoming Test) is based on the arrival of a material/item/product where the Incoming Testing check box is selected. It is part of the Receiving and Receiving QC processes as well as the recertification process.

The MARS Requests page is accessed from the Supply Chain menu. Select the Stock Room Operations menu item, then the Receiving menu item, and then the MARS Requests menu item. The MARS Requests page opens in its own tab.

You have no page to create a MARS request for your company. If you discover a need for a MARS Request please work with the HGI Customer Service Representative to have it added to tcmIS.

Figure 1

In the upper panel you may specify the records needed.

  • You may provide something to at least partially identify the records you want using the Search: boxes. Your search may be specified by Test Request, Item ID, Customer Part, Lot Status, or Receipt ID.) See Search functions for more information.
  • You may choose to narrow the search by selecting a specific Status from the Request Status drop-down list (or leave it at the default All).
  • You may limit the search to items that have been in the lab for at least a specific period by entering a number in the Over ___ Day(s) in Lab text box.

Click the Search button to start the search. Click the Create Excel button to export the data in Excel format.

Figure 2

A right click on any column of any record will display a context menu where you may choose to view details about that specific MARS record by clicking on the View MARS Detail menu item. tcmIS will open a child window to display the MARS Details page.
