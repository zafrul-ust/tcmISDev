Work Area Stock Turns Report

The Work Area Stock Turns Report page is used to produce a usage report of material over the most recent six-month period with a calculation of the six-month total usage, average weekly usage, and projection of annual inventory turnover given the established stocking level. The Work Area Stock Turns Report page can be accessed by selecting Inventory from the Reports menu and the selecting Work Area Stock Turns Report. The Work Area Stock Turns Report page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select the Division, Facility Group, and Facility from their respective lists. In some cases the default values are all that are available to you. Choose the Work Area Group from its list, or leave the default value of All, and the specific Work Area if appropriate.

You may use the Facility with Bin check box to limit the search to those facilities.

Click the Search button to see the report. The numbers are arranged in the right-most columns (shown below).

  • The M1 through M6 columns display the quantity dispensed from the cabinet in each of the previous six months (from oldest to most recent).
  • The Half-year Total is simply the sum of the previous six month totals.
  • The Avg User per Week is the Half-year Total divided by the 26 weeks in the six months.
  • The Turns is an annualized projection based on the SL (stocking level). For the given SL and at the average user per week, Turns is the number of times the inventory will be completely replaced.

Click the Create Excel File button to export the results of the report in Excel format.
