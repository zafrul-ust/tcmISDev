Glossary

Buy Order (BO): An order to purchase material generated by tcmIS that directs a buyer to place a purchase order with a supplier. Buy Orders are generated as a result of a Material Request (MR) by a client for an OOR Material or as a result of a MM material where onhand inventory has fallen below the minimum stocking level . A Buy Order is sometimes also referred to as Purchase Request (PR).

Item: A material purchased by the Haas TCM Procurement Department through tcmIS. An item identifies a material by description, manufacturer, kit components and unit size. Note: Items are purchased by Haas TCM. Clients order materials as Part Numbers through their respective catalogs. An item can service many parts in different catalogs. Likewise, a part can be serviced by more than one item.

Item Type: A categorization of material according to how the material is stocked and/or delivered to the client.

Line Item: An individual line on a Purchase Order or Material Request which represents a specific material.

Lot: An identifier assigned to material by the manufacturer that is used to identify the material by date of manufacturer and batch.

Material Request (MR): A request for materials by a client originating from tcmIS. A Material Request can be either for an Order On Request material or a Min/Max material.

Min/Max(MM): An Item Type that is stocked by Haas TCM for Just-In-Time delivery. Buy Orders and Purchase Orders are created for Min/Max materials when inventory levels fall below the Reorder Point.

Obsolete Material: Material for which no Material Request has been made within 270 days. Also material whaich is no longer produced by the manufacturer.

Order On Request (OOR): An Item Type which is not routinely stocked by Haas TCM. A separate Buy Order and Purchase Order are created for each OOR.

Purchase Order: An order placed with a supplier for materials. A Purchase Order may have multiple Line Items for different materials (though typically a separate Purchase Order is generated for each new material).

Receipt ID: A unique identifier for materials received into tcmIS. Typically, a Receipt ID is generated for each Lot of materials received.

Receiving Checklist: A checklist of information regarding a Purchase Order. The checklist is used to confirm the identity of incoming material and to record data on lot numbers, quantity, and date of receipt.

Reorder Point (RP): The inventory level of a material below which an automated Buy Order is generated by tcmIS. The inventory level includes the on-hand quantity, unfilled Material Requests, and pending Purchase Orders. The Buy Order will be for a quantity sufficient to restore the inventory level of materials to the Stocking Level.

Return Material Authorization (RMA): An authorization by a supplier to return material previously shipped to Haas TCM.

Scrap Material: Material that is expired, damaged, or otherwise no longer meets client requirements for continued use.

Stocking Level (SL): The maximum quantity of a material in inventory considering the on-hand quantity, unfilled Material Requests, and pending Purchase Orders.

Supplier ID: A unique identifier for each supplier that is available in tcmIS.
