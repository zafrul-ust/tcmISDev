tcmIS Navigation

The tcmIS Application begins with a cascading menu system that provides access to all (and only) the pages for which you have permission.

Figure 1

Each time you use the menu system to navigate to a particular page, a tabbed window is opened. You may switch from one open page (tab) to another by clicking on the tab of the page you want to view. Each page will maintain the data as you last left it.

Clicking on the small x on the right end of any tab will cause that tabbed window to close. Or, you may right click on the tab and select the Close Tab item on the menu. (See below.)

Figure 2: One tab open
Figure 3: Two tabs open

For convenience you may control which tabs open automatically whenever you start tcmIS. Right click on the tab of the currently active tab and you will get a menu to (a) Close Tab; (b) Add Tab to Startup; or, (c) Remove Tab from Startup.

You may notice that some tabs have been placed in your startup already. You may manage these tabs in the same way.
