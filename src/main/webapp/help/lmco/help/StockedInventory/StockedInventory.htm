Stocked Inventory

The Stocked Inventory page displays information about on hand and on order inventory stocked for BAE Systems facilities at HGI hubs.

The Stocked Inventory page can be accessed from the Report menu by selecting Inventory, then Stocked Inventory. The Stocked Inventory page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select the facility and inventory group where the material is stored, from their respective boxes. In some cases there may be only a single inventory group in the list.

You may narrow your search using other criteria on the form.

  • Enter text associated with the desired material in the Search Text box. Search text can include all or part of any of BAE Systems part number, HGI item ID, packaging, trade name, manufacturer, manufacturer's part number, or material description.
    • The more specific the information entered, the more the search is narrowed. Multiple words as a logical expression can also be used to refine a search (separate words by and, or, not; for example, prc AND 1750).
    • To search for all parts leave the Search Text box blank.
  • An inventory search can also be limited to those chemicals whose shelf life expires within or after a certain time frame by entering a number of days in the Expires Within or Expires After text boxes. (Be careful using both that you do not inadvertantly enter contradictory limits, e.g., expires within 90 days and expires after 120 days.)
  • On order items that are due to be delivered within a specific number of days can be located by entering a number of days in the On Order Arrives within text box.

Click the Search button to see the inventory of active parts for the selected inventory group that match your criteria.

Click the Create Excel button on the page to export data in Excel format.
