Work Area Scan Score Report

The Work Area Scan Score Report page can be accessed by selecting Inventory from the Reports menu and the selecting Work Area Scan Score Report. The Work Area Scan Score Report page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

There are six parameters which may be used to describe the Work Area Scan Score report you want although for some of them you may have only a single option:

  • Division may be chosen from the list.
  • Facility Group may be chosen from the list.
    • Facility may be chosen if a specific Facility Group has been selected, from the list.
  • Work Area Group may be chosen from the list.
    • Work Area may be chosen if a specific Work Area Group has been selected, from the list.
  • Select the Facility with Bin check box to limit the report to only those facilities which have stocked Work Areas (or Cabinets).

When you have made your option choices, click the Search button to run the report.

tcmIS will display the search result in a data grid. It displays a count of scans and a ’score’ (percentage of all items/bins scanned) for each Division, Facility Group, Facility, Work Area Group, or Work Area that meets the search criteria. The counts and scores cover several overlapping recent time periods: the most recent 14 days, the most recent 30 days, the most recent 90 days, and the most recent 180 days.

Click the Create Excel button to export the results of the report in Excel format.

Figure 2

A right-click on any record will display a context menu item to Print the report. Please note that the report is wide. Adjust your print settings to Landscape to fit the printed report to your paper.
