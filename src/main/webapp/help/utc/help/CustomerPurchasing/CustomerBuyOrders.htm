Customer Buy Orders

The Customer Buy Orders page is used to view and manage tcmIS customer buy orders for UTC PO’s sent directly to suppliers. Entering these PO’s into tcmIS makes it possible for HGI to continue to receive, manage, and issue the inventory as usual.

The Customer Buy Orders page can be accessed from the Order Management menu by hovering the mouse over Customer Purchasing, then selecting the Customer Buy Orders menu item. The page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

To locate the PO or PO’s:

  • Select a buyer name from the Entered By drop-down list to see all purchase orders entered by the selected buyer, or select Any Buyer to display all purchase orders regardless of the buyer.
  • Select the inventory group from the Inventory Group drop-down list or leave the default All selection.
  • You may limit the search to a specific PO or set of PO’s using the Search by boxes. See Search functions for more information.

Click the Search button to display the records.

You may edit any record using one of the last three columns on the data grid.

  • The PO quantity is displayed for each item. The quantity may be changed by editing the number in the Qty field. However, please note that changing the quantity on this screen will not close open buy orders on the Customer Purchasing page.
  • The Promised Date can be edited in the text box in the Promised Date column. Click the blue diamond icon () to open a small calendar from which you may select the new date.
  • The Supplier information can be edited in the text box in the Supplier column.

You must click the Update link to save any changes.
