Stocked Inventory

The Stocked Inventory page displays information about on hand and on order inventory stocked for UTC facilities at Haas TCM hubs. The Stocked Inventory page can be accessed from the Report menu by selecting Inventory. Then select Stocked Inventory. The Stocked Inventory page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select a facility and inventory group from the corresponding drop-down boxes to limit the search to only those items associated with parts for the selected facility and inventory group. Click Search to see inventory of all active parts for the selected inventory group.

Figure 2

To narrow an inventory search further, enter a UTC part number or material description in the Search Text box. Click the Search button.

Figure 3

An inventory search can also be limited to those chemicals whose shelf life expires within or after a certain time frame by entering a number of days in the Expires Within or Expires After text boxes.

Figure 4

On order items that are due to be delivered within a specific number of days can be located by entering a number of days in the On Order Arrives within text box.

Figure 5

To find out more about the inventory of a particular item, right-click on the part and select Inventory Details.

Figure 6

On hand inventory is displayed including the manufacturer's lot number and expiration date.

On order items are displayed including the Haas TCM purchase order number and the estimated date the material will be ready for shipment to UTC.

Figure 7

To view a graph of inventory for the material for the last two months, right-click on the part and select Inventory - 2 months.

Figure 8

The inventory level is shown in red. The reorder point is shown in blue. The maximum stocking level is shown in black.

Figure 9

To view a graph of inventory for the material for the last year, right-click on the part and select Inventory - 1 year.

Figure 10

The inventory level is shown in red. The reorder point is shown in blue. The maximum stocking level is shown in black.

Figure 11

To view a graph of material request lead times for the material, right-click on the part and select MR Lead Time.

Figure 12

Each delivery from Haas TCM to UTC is plotted on the graph. The tenth, fiftieth, and ninetieth percentile is also plotted.

Figure 13

To view a graph of supply lead times for the material, right-click on the part and select Supply Lead Time.

Figure 14

Each delivery from the supplier to the Haas TCM hub is plotted on the graph. The tenth, fiftieth, and ninetieth percentile is also plotted.

Figure 15

To view details on delivery patterns of the material, right-click on the part and select Issues.

Figure 16

Deliveries from the Haas TCM hub to UTC are plotted on the graph.

Figure 17

To view details on receipt patterns of the material, right-click on the part and select Receipts.

Figure 18

Receipts from suppliers to the Haas TCM hub are plotted on the graph.

Figure 19

Click Create Excel File on the Stocked Inventory page to export the data to Excel.
