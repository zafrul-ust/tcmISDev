Customer Buy Orders

The Customer Buy Orders page displays a summary of UTC purchase orders that have been entered in tcmIS so that Haas TCM can continue to receive, issue, and manage the inventory as usual, but UTC’s buyers issue purchase orders outside of tcmIS. This page can be used to update the purchase order information entered in tcmIS. The Customer Buy Orders page can be launched from the Order Management menu by selecting Customer Purchasing. Then select Customer Buy Orders. The page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select a name from the Entered By drop-down box to see all purchase orders entered by the selected buyer. Select Any Buyer to display all purchase orders regardless of the buyer.

Select the inventory group from the Inventory Group drop-down list.

The UTC PO number can be entered in the text box to display a specific PO. Leave the text box blank to display all POs. Click Search.

Figure 2

The total quantity on the PO for each item is displayed. The quantity can be changed on this page by updating the Qty field and clicking the Update link above the data grid. However, please note that changing the quantity on this screen will not close open buy orders on the Customer Purchasing page.

The promised date can be updated by changing the date in the Promised Date column. Click the blue diamond to select the date from the calendar. You may update the supplier as well. Click the Update link to save any changes.
