Customer Purchasing

The Customer Purchasing page enables purchasing to be performed by UTC. Haas TCM can continue to receive, issue, and manage the inventory as usual, but UTC’s buyers issue purchase orders outside of tcmIS. The inventory is owned by UTC and there is never any payable generated in tcmIS. The Customer Purchasing page can be launched from the Order Management menu by selecting Customer Purchasing. Then select Customer Purchasing. The page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

UTC buyers can retrieve buy orders, or purchase requests, from the Customer Purchasing page. A buy order is a request to purchase material generated by tcmIS that directs a buyer to place a purchase order with a supplier. Buy orders are generated as a result of a material request. Buy orders are also generated to replenish stock for MM items.

To see all open buy orders for a facility, select the inventory group from the Inventory Group drop-down box and click Search. Select the check box labeled Show only Open Requests to view only open buy orders.

You may search for a specific buy order by entering the UTC PO number in the Search box and clicking Search.

Figure 2

The Date Created, Purchase Request Number, Haas Item ID, and Item Description are displayed. The requested quantity and packaging are displayed as well. This information tells the UTC purchaser how much of a specific material tcmIS would like them to purchase.

Please note that there may be multiple buy orders for the same item. These will be grouped together by default. Multiple buy orders can be grouped and placed together on the same UTC PO.

The UTC purchaser should use the Customer Purchasing page to record the UTC PO number and the quantity for each buy order. Enter the UTC PO number in the PO field. Verify that the quantity purchased is the same as the quantity in the Qty field. If it is different, update the Qty field. Please note that the quantity entered should be the quantity on the customer PO for that buy order, not the total quantity for that item on the UTC PO. Enter the supplier's promised delivery date in the Promised Date column. Click the blue diamond to select the date from the calendar. Enter the supplier name in the Supplier column. Check the OK box on the left side of the but order line. Multiple buy orders can be updated at one time. Click the Process link above the data grid to save the data.
