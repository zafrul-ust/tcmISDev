Deliveries

The Deliveries page is used to view information about deliveries and access documentation associated with the delivery like certifications, receiving checklists, or supplier packing lists. The Deliveries page can be accessed from the Reports menu by selecting Deliveries. The Deliveries page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select a facility from the Facility drop-down list to see deliveries from Haas TCM to the selected facility.

Locate specific deliveries by selecting a search criteria from the Search by drop-down list. Select contains, starts with, ends with, or is to indicate what type of search to perform. Enter the corresponding data in the text box and click Search.

The tcmIS Receipt ID can be found on the label of all materials delivered from a Haas TCM Hub. You may locate deliveries of a specific Receipt ID by selecting Receipt from the Search by drop-down list and entering the Receipt ID in the text box. Click the Search button. All deliveries of the Receipt ID entered are displayed.

Figure 2

The manufacturer's lot or batch number can be found on the label of all materials delivered from a Haas TCM Hub. You may locate deliveries of a specific manufacurer's lot number by selecting Lot from the Search by drop-down list and entering the lot number in the text box. Click the Search button. All deliveries of the manufacturer's lot number entered are displayed.

Figure 3

The tcmIS Material Request number and/or MR Line number can also be used to locate a delivery. You may locate deliveries of a specific Material Request by selecting MR-Line from the Search by drop-down list and entering the Material Request ID in the text box. Click the Search button. All deliveries against the Material Request ID entered are displayed.

Figure 4

To locate a specific line item on a Material Request, select MR-Line from the Search by drop-down list and enter the Material Request ID followed by a dash (-) and the line number in the text box. Click the Search button. All deliveries of the MR Line entered are displayed.

Figure 5

The UTC part number can be found on the label of all materials delivered from a Haas TCM Hub. You may locate deliveries of a specific part number by selecting Part Number from the Search by drop-down list and entering the part number in the text box. Click the Search button. All deliveries of the UTC part number entered are displayed.

Figure 6

You may locate deliveries of a specific material by using the UTC part description. Select Part Desc from the Search by drop-down list and enter the description in the text box. Click the Search button. All deliveries containing the part description entered are displayed. Be sure to select contains as the search criteria. You may use % as a wildcard when entering the item description.

Figure 7

The tcmIS Item ID can be found on the label of all materials delivered from a Haas TCM Hub. You may locate deliveries of a specific Item ID by selecting Item from the Search by drop-down list and entering the Item ID in the text box. Click the Search button. All deliveries of the Item ID entered are displayed.

Figure 8

You may locate deliveries of a specific material by using the tcmIS item description. Select Item Desc from the Search by drop-down list and enter the description in the text box. Click the Search button. All deliveries containing the item description entered are displayed. Be sure to select contains as the search criteria. You may use % as a wildcard when entering the item description.

Figure 9

Click the link in the Receipt column to view any documents that were uploaded into tcmIS by the Haas TCM Hub when the materials were received at the hub. These documents may include receiving checklists, packing lists, certificates of conformance, or certificates of analysis.

Figure 10

Click Create Excel File to export the data on the Deliveries page to Excel.
