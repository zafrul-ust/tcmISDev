TcmIS Navigation

The tcmIS Application consists of a menu system and individual tabs that correspond to each entry in the menu.

Figure 1

The menu system shows only the pages that you have permission to view.

Figure 2

Upon selecting any item in the menu system, tcmIS initiates a new tab and starts the corresponding page in the tab. Note that you may move freely between tabs and each page will “remember” the data on the page as you last left it.

Figure 3

If you right-click your mouse while pointing at a tab, you may add the tab to the startup procedure which will cause that tab to automatically initiate when you next login. You may notice that some tabs have been placed in your startup already. You may remove tabs from the startup procedure in the same way.

Click the X in the right corner of the tab to close a tab. You may also right-click on the tab and select Close Tab.
