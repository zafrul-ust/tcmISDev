Invoice Correction

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Invoice Correction process is used to credit and rebill invoices for Distribution Hubs in tcmIS. The Invoice Corrections process should be started from the Invoice Print page. The Invoice Print page can be accessed from the Distribution menu by selecting Invoice Print. The Invoice Print page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Locate the invoice which needs correction. For more information on searching the Invoice Print page, please see the Invoice Print help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/InvoicePrint/InvoicePrint.htm.

Right-click on the invoice line to correct and select Correct Invoice.

Figure 2

All material lines on the invoice are displayed as well as line charges and header charges.

To remove taxes from an invoice, it is first necessary to add a Tax Exemption Certificate to the Customer. This can be accomplished from the Customer Details page. For more information on entering Tax Exemption Certificates, please see the Edit Customer help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/EditCustomer.htm.

Once the Tax Exemption Certificate has been added, no changes are required on the Invoice Correction page. Simply resubmit the Invoice by clicking the Submit link at the top left. You may also add taxes to an invoice in the same manner. Remove the Tax Exemption Certificate Customer and then submit an invoice correction.

To change the material price on an invoice line, enter the corrected price in the Adjusted Unit Price column. Click the Save link in the Invoice Line section to save changes without submitting the Invoice Correction. If there are no changes to the Line Charges or Header Charges click the Submit link.

Figure 3

To change the price or remove Line Charges, right click on the invoice line and select Line Charges.

Figure 4

To change the price on a Line Charge simply enter the correct price in the Price Column.

To remove a charge from the invoice check the box in the column labeled Marked For Deletion.

Click the Add Line link to add additional Line Charges to the selected invoice line. For more information on adding charges, please see the Additional Charges help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/OrderEntry/AdditionalCharges.htm.

Click the Update link to save changes. Click the Close link to return to the Invoice Correction page.

Figure 5

The changes are displayed on the Invoice Correction page. Click the Save link in the Invoice Line section to save changes without submitting the Invoice Correction. To Submit the Invoice Correction click the Submit link.

Figure 6

To change the price or remove Header Charges, right click on the Edit link in the Header Charges section. Edit the Header charges in the same manner described above for Line Charges.

Figure 7

To Submit the Invoice Correction click the Submit link.

After an Invoice Correction has been submitted, click the Print link to preview the corrective invoice. If this invoice is not correct, click the Undo link. The invoice is deleted and you can continue to correct the invoice.

If the invoice is correct click the Approve link.

Figure 8

When an Invoice Correction is approved, the original invoice is credited and a new invoice is generated. All three transactions can be viewed on the Invoice Print page by searching by MR or Customer PO as shown below.
