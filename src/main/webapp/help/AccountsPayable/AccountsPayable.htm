Accounts Payable

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Accounts Payable page facilitates matching of supplier invoices to Haas TCM Purchase Orders and Receipts by the Accounts Payable team. Once supplier invoices have been matched, the invoice is vouchered and paid. Haas TCM employees can use the Accounts Payable page to view the status of the matching process. The Accounts Payable page can be accessed by selecting Accounts Payable from the Accounts Payable menu. The Accounts Payable page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Enter the Purchase Order number in the PO text box and click to look up a purchase order.

Figure 2

Select an invoice number from the Invoice drop-sown list. You may view a summary of the invoice by clicking the Invoice link.

Figure 3

Click the Expediting Notes link to view notes from buyers or expeditors regarding this purchase order.

Figure 4

A summary of all Purchase Order lines is displayed near the middle of the Accounts Payable page.

Figure 5

Most Haas TCM employees have view only permissions to the Accounts Payable page and utilize this page to check the status of a supplier invoice in the matching process. All invoices that have been matched to the Purchase Order are displayed in the Invoice - PO Matching section of the Accounts Payable page. Click Show Only Open Invoices to display only invoices that have not been matched. Click Show All to return to viewing all invoices for this purchase order.

Figure 6

All invoices that have been matched to a Receipt ID in tcmIS are displayed in the Invoice - Receipt Matching section of the Accounts Payable page. Click Show Only Receipts to Match to display only receipts that have not been matched to an invoice. Click Show All to return to viewing all receipts for this purchase order.

Figure 7

The Accounts Payable team has permission to enter and match supplier invoices on the Accounts Payable page. To add an invoice to match, click the New button.

Figure 8

Enter the invoice number in the Invoice text box. Select the currency from the Currency drop-down list. Enter the invoice date date the invoice was received in the Inv Date and Date Received boxes. Click the blue diamonds to select the date from the calendar. Enter the invoice total in the Inv Amt text box. Select the payment terms on the invoice from the Terms drop-down list. Enter any notes in the Comments box and click the OK button.

Figure 9

The data entered is populated on the Accounts Payable page. Click Add Line in the Invoice - PO Matching section to enter an invoice line to match.

Figure 10

Select the invoice number from the Invoice drop-down box. Enter the invoice quantity and the unit price from the supplier invoice. Click OK.

Figure 11

The invoice line is populated on the Accounts Payable page. Click Undo to remove the line. You may also add invoice corrections or credits in the same manner by clicking Add Correction or Add RMA (Credit) respectively.

Figure 12

If there are receipts in tcmIS for the Purchase Order, they will be displayed in the Invoice - Receipt Matching section of the Accounts Payable page. Select the invoice number from the Invoice drop-down list and enter the invoiced quantity in the Qty text box. Click Update to save changes and approve the invoice if all matching is complete.

Figure 13

The invoice is now approved in tcmIS.
