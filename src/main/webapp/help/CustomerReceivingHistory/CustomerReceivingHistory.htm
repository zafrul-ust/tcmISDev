Customer Receiving History

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Customer Receiving History page is used to view all receipts into an inventory group made during a specific date range. This page is especially useful for customers that submit unique PO numbers and/or release numbers for each order. The Customer Receiving History page can be accessed by selecting Stocking Room Operations from the Supply Chain menu. Select Receiving then Customer Receiving History. The Customer Receiving History page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select the hub and inventory group from the corresponding drop-down boxes.

Enter the start date of the date range to view receipts in the DOR Begin box by clicking in the text box and selecting the date from the calendar. Enter the end date of the date range to view receipts in the DOR End box by clicking in the text box and selecting the date from the calendar.

Click the Search button to display all receipts made in the specified date range for the selected inventory group.

Figure 2

To locate all receipts made against a specific Haas TCM purchase order, select Haas PO from the Search by drop-down list and enter the Haas TCM purchase order number in the text box to the right of the Search by box. Click Search.

Figure 3

To locate all receipts made for a specific customer purchase order, select Customer PO from the Search by drop-down list and enter the customer purchase order number in the text box to the right of the Search by box. Click Search.

Figure 4

To locate all receipts made for a specific customer release number, select Release No from the Search by drop-down list and enter the customer release number in the text box to the right of the Search by box. Click Search.

Figure 5

To locate all receipts made for a specific customer part number, select Part # from the Search by drop-down list and enter the customer part number in the text box to the right of the Search by box. Click Search.

Figure 6

To locate all receipts made for a specific Item ID, select Item from the Search by drop-down list and enter the Item ID in the text box to the right of the Search by box. Click Search.

Figure 7

The report can be displayed using the customer's unit of measure or the Haas TCM item unit of measure. Select UOS from the Unit of Measure drop-down box to run the report using the customer's unit of measure, also known as unit of sale. Select Item's UOM from the Unit of Measure drop-down box to run the report using the Haas TCM unit of measure, typically an individual container or "each."

Figure 8

The results can be sorted by Haas PO number, customer PO number, customer release number, Item ID, or customer part number by selecting the desired sort option from the Sort drop-down box. Click Search to sort.

Click Create Excel File to export the results of the Customer Receiving History page into Excel.
