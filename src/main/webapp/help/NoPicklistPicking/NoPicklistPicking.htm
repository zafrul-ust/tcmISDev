No Picklist Picking

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

Picking, Picking QC, and Ship Confirm can be combined into one step known as No Picklist Picking. Although this method of performing the shipping process was designed specifically for Haas TCM hubs that service Seagate, other client operated hubs may elect to use this method as well. The No Picklist Picking page is used to perform the picking and shipping process in tcmIS. This page can be accessed from the Supply Chain Menu. Under Stock Room Operations, select Delivery and then No Picklist Picking.

Figure 1

The No Picklist Picking page is very similar to the Picking Page. Select the Hub for which Picking will be performed from the Hub drop-down box. Click Search to display all Material Requests which are ready to be picked.

To display picks for a specific facility, select the desired facility from the Facility drop-down box and click Search.

To locate a specific Material Request which is ready for Picking, enter the MR number in the MR Line text box and click Search.

Figure 2

Enter the quantity picked in the Qty Picked column on the far right of the No Picklist Picking page next to the Receipt ID that is being delivered. Click Confirm.

Figure 3

Note that tcmIS will recommend which Receipt ID to pick based on a First In First Out (FIFO) algorithm based on the Date of Receipt as entered during the Receiving process. You may elect to issue a different Receipt ID if needed. Simply record the Qty Picked next to the appropriate Receipt ID, as long as the total picked is equal to the MR Total Quantity.

After the MR is confirmed, you may print a Packing List, Bill of Lading, or Box Labels by clicking the corresponding links above the data grid.
