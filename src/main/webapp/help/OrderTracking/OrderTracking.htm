Order Tracking

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Order Tracking page allows internal customers to determine the status of a Purchase Order raised to a Distribution Hub in tcmIS. The Order Tracking page can be accessed from the Distribution menu by selecting Order Tracking. The Order Tracking page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

The Order Tracking page allows Haas Group Buyers to search for materials ordered from Distribution Hubs in tcmIS and view their status. First select the company the PO was issued from in the Customer list.

Select an order status from Status list. The default is to search for Open orders. Select All if the order status is not known. Select Complete to search only fully delivered orders.

You may limit your search by entering dates in the Order Date From and Order Date To fields. Click anywhere in the text boxes and select a date from the calendar.

Alternately, you may limit your search by entering dates in the Promised Ship Date From and Promised Ship Date To fields. Click anywhere in the text boxes and select a date from the calendar.

Click the Search button to view all orders which meet the entered search criteria. Click the Create Excel button to export this report in Excel format.

Figure 2

If you know your Material Request (Sales Order) number for the Distribution Hub, select MR and is from the Search drop-down lists and enter the MR Number in the text box. Click Search.

Figure 3

If you know the tcmIS Item ID, select Catalog Item and contains from the Search drop-down lists and enter the Item ID in the text box. Click Search.

Figure 4

If you know your PO Number number, select Customer PO and is from the Search drop-down lists and enter your PO Number in the text box. Click Search.

Figure 5

To learn the status of an order, right-click the anywhere on the line and select MR Line Allocation.

Figure 6
The MR Allocation Report is displayed. The order status is listed in the Status column. The order status will be one of the following:
  • Available: The part is available for delivery and is in process of being picked and shipped.
  • Missing Certs: The material is in the warehouse but not has the necessary certifications to ship the material.
  • NonConforming: The material is in the warehouse but is not the correct material of has inadequate shelf life.
  • On Order: The part is on order. A purchase order number and the estimated ship date will be listed.
  • In Purchasing: A purchase order has not yet been issued.

This screen can be exported to Excel by clicking the Create Excel button.

If an order has been delivered, the Packing List (Certificate of Conformity) is also available. Right-click anywhere on the line and select Display Packing List to see a copy of the Packing List that was sent with the shipment.

Figure 11

If an order has been delivered, a copy of the Invoice is also available. Right-click anywhere on the line and select Display Invoice to see a copy of the Invoice for the shipment.
