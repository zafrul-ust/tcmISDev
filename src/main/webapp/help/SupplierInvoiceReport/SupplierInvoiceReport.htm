Supplier Invoice Report

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Supplier Invoice Report page provides information on the status of supplier invoices. The Supplier Invoice Report page can be accessed by selecting Supplier Invoice Report from the Accounts Payable menu. The Supplier Invoice Report page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

To view a specific supplier invoice, enter the supplier invoice number in the Invoice text box. Click Search.

Figure 2

To limit results to invoice for a specific supplier, enter the supplier name in the Supplier text box. Click Search.

Figure 3

To look up invoices assigned to a specific Accounts Payable team member, select a name from the Name drop-down list. Click Search.

Figure 4

To view supplier invoices in a specific status, select the appropriate status from the Status drops-down list and click Search.

It is advisable to enter a number of days in the Invoice Age box to limit the result.

Figure 5

Results can be sorted by PO and Invoice, Invoice, Supplier and Invoice, Invoice Date or Approved Date by selecting the desired sort option from the Sort By drop-down list.

Check the box labeled Show Only With Available Receipts to view only invoices which have receipts in tcmIS. Click Search.

Figure 6

Check the box labeled Show Only Invoices to be QCed to view only invoices which have been approved but need a secondary quality check before final approval in tcmIS. Enter a date range in the Approved From and To text boxes. Click the blue diamonds to select the date from the calendar. Click Search.

Figure 7

You may combine any of the search criteria above to locate specific groups of invoices.

Click the link in the PO column to display the purchase order. Click the link in the Invoice column to display the invoice on the Accounts Payable page.
