MR History

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The MR History page is used to look up and view Material Requests. This page can be used to view late orders, gross margin exceptions, and back orders. The MR History page can be accessed from the Reports menu by selecting Orders and MR History. The MR History page is displayed in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select the operating entity, hub, and inventory group for which you would like to search. Or choose My Entities, My Hubs, and My Inventory Groups to see MRs for all inventory groups which you have permission to view.

Select a status from the Status list box. Select All to see MRs, Quotes, or Scratch Pads in any status. Keep in mind that an MR is not considered complete until all lines on the MR have shipped complete.

You may limit the results by order date. Click in the Order Date From and To text boxes and select dates from the calendar.

You may limit the results by promised ship date as well. Click in the Promised Ship Date From and To text boxes and select dates from the calendar.

Click Search.

Figure 2

You may search for Material Requests for a specific customer by clicking the labeled Customer. Use the Customer Search page to look up the customer and populate the Customer field on the Order Entry Lookup page. Click Search.

For more details on the Customer Lookup process, see the Customer Lookup help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/CustomerLookup2.htm.

Figure 3

Check the box labeled Show Late Orders Only to view orders that have not been delivered and the promised ship date is in the past.

Figure 4

Check the box labeled Show Gross Margin Exceptions to view orders where the gross margin is outside the limits set for the Operating Entity.

Figure 5

Check the box labeled Show Back Orders Only to view orders which are on back order with a supplier or on a open Buy Order.

Figure 6

You may search for a specific Material Request by selecting MR from the Search drop-down list and entering the MR Number in the text box. Click Search.

Figure 7

You may search for all MRs for a specific Item by selecting Catalog Item from the Search drop-down list and entering the Item ID in the text box. Click Search.

Figure 8

You may search for a specific Customer PO Number by selecting Customer PO from the Search drop-down list and entering the Customer's PO Number in the text box. Click Search.

Figure 9

You may view the MR by right-clicking on the line and selecting Open. The MR is opened in a new tab.

Figure 10

To view more details regarding the status of the entire order, right-click on a line and select MR Allocation. To view more details regarding the status of a specific MR line, right-click on the MR line and select MR Line Allocation.

Figure 11
The order status is listed in the Status column. The order status may be one of the following:
  • Available: The part is available for delivery and is in process of being picked and shipped.
  • Missing Certs: The material is in the warehouse but not has the necessary certifications to ship the material.
  • NonConforming: The material is in the warehouse but is not the correct material of has inadequate shelf life.
  • On Order: The part is on order. A purchase order number and the estimated ship date will be listed.
  • In Purchasing: A purchase order has not yet been issued.
  • Delivered: Material has been Ship Confirmed.

This screen can be exported to Excel by clicking the Create Excel File button.

Figure 12

If the material is in Delivered status, the carrier and tracking number is displayed. You may retrieve copies of certifications, MSDS, Packing Lists, and and Invoices. Right click on the line and select the appropriate action.
