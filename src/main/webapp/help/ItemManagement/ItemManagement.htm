Item Management

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Item Management page is used to create buy orders for counting inventory groups. The Item Management page can be accessed from Supply Chain Menu by selecting Stock Room Operations. Then select Counting and Item Management. The page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

The Item Management page is used by Haas TCM representatives to order Min/Max materials, or "force a buy." MM materials with stocking levels of 0/0 must be ordered from this page. MM materials with stocking levels other than 0/0 may need to be ordered from this page if the demand for the material is more than usual and the current stocking levels are not set to accommodate this increase in demand.

If individual purchase orders for each order are provided by the customer, then the Item Management page should not be used to create Buy Orders. Instead, Material Requests should be created in the tcmIS Java Application.

To show all materials that may be ordered for a facility, select the desired hub from the Program drop-down list and select the desired facility from the Plant drop-down list and click Search. All MM items are displayed in addition to any OOR items for which there is on hand inventory.

Figure 2

To locate a specific material on the Item Management page, enter the customer part number or Item ID in the search box. Select the corresponding search criteria from the Search drop-down list. and click Search.

Figure 3

You may also enter a description of the material in the text box and click to lookup the Item ID.

Figure 4

Select the desired item by clicking anywhere on the line and click Ok. If the item is not found, you may continue to search by entering text in the Search Text box and clicking Search.

The item ID of the selected material will now appear in the Item text box. Click Search to display details for the item. Enter the quantity needed in the Replenish Qty box. Be sure to verify the unit of measure in the Description field and use the appropriate quantity for the specified unit of measure. For example, if the unit of measure is 275 gal tote, the Replenish Qty should be 1 and not 275 for a single tote.

Enter the date the material should be delivered in the Need Date box. Click on the diamond to select the date from the calendar. Enter any notes to your buyer in the Comments box. Check the box labeled OK and click Update to process your order. The quantity in the In Purchasing column will be incremented by the Replenish Qty. A Buy Order is generated as a result of orders placed from the Item Management page. Check to Buy Orders page to view the buy order that was created.

Figure 5

Items can be made inactive by changing the status from Active to Inactive and clicking Update.

Uncheck the Show Active Only box and click Search to show inactive items. Inactive items can be activated by changing the status to Active and clicking Update.
