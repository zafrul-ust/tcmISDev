Order Entry Lookup

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

Click the Lookup link at the top left of the Order Entry page to search for and view existing Material Requests, Quotes, or Scratch Pads.

Figure 1

The Order Entry Lookup page is displayed in a new window.

Select the operating entity, hub, and inventory group for which you would like to search. Or choose My Entities, My Hubs, and My Inventory Groups to see MRs, Quotes, or Scratch Pads for all inventory groups which you have permission to view.

Select a status from the Status list box. Select All to see MRs, Quotes, or Scratch Pads in any status.

Check the box labeled MR to include Material Requests in the search results. Check the box labeled Quote to include Quotes in the search results. Check the box labeled Scratch Pad to include Scratch Pads in the search results.

You may limit the results by date as well. Click in the Order/Quote Date text box and select a date from the calendar. Click Search.

Figure 2

To limit the results to MRs, Quotes, or Scratch Pads entered by a specific person click the to the right of the Entered By field. If you are a CSR, your name appears here by default.

Enter the the name you are looking for in the Search text box and click the Search button. Select the desired user by clicking anywhere on the line. Click the Return Selected User link.

Figure 3

The Entered By field on the Order Entry Lookup window is now populated with the selected user's name. Click Search to locate all Material Requests, Quotes, or Scratch Pads entered by the specified user. Click the Clear button to clear the Entered By field. Click Search to locate all Material Requests, Quotes, or Scratch Pads entered by any user.

Figure 4

You may search for Material Requests, Quotes, or Scratch Pads for a specific customer by clicking the labeled Customer. Use the Customer Search page to look up the customer and populate the Customer field on the Order Entry Lookup page. Click Search.

For more details on the Customer Lookup process, see the Customer Lookup help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/CustomerLookup2.htm.

Figure 5

You may search for Quotes, Scratch Pads, or Material Requests using the Quote, SP, or MR number. Select SP/Q/MR from the Search drop-down list and select is. Enter the Quote, SP, or MR number in the Search text box. Click the Search button.

Figure 6

You may search for Material Requests, Quotes, or Scratch Pads using the Item ID. Select Catalog Item from the Search drop-down list and enter the Item ID in the Search text box. Click the Search button.

Figure 7

You may search for Material Requests, Quotes, or Scratch Pads using the customer's reference or PO number. Select Customer PO from the Search drop-down list and select is. Enter the customer's PO number in the Search text box. Click the Search button.

Figure 8

Once a Material Request, Quote, or Scratch Pad is located, you may open it by right-clicking anywhere on the line and selecting Open.

You may also highlight the line to open by clicking anywhere on the line and then click the Open Quote, Open MR, or Open Scratch Pad link above the data grid.

Figure 9

The Material Request, Quote, or Scratch Pad is opened in a new tab.
