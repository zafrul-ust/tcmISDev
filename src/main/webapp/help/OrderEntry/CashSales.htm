Cash Sales

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Cash Sales process is used when a new customer needs a quote or material request and is not yet added to tcmIS. Cash Sales may also be used when a customer is over their credit limit. Cash sales may also be used to issue material out of inventory if it is to be consumed by the business. You may begin the Cash Sales process from the Scratch Pad by clicking the labeled Customer.

Look up the proper Cash Sales account by entering the account name in the Customer Name text box. Click the Search button. Select the Cash Sales account by clicking anywhere on the line. Click the Return Selected Customer & Ship To link to populate the Customer field on the Scratch Pad with the Cash Sales account.

Figure 1

Enter the customer's contact information in the Contact, Telephone, Fax, and Email text boxes.

Figure 2

You should click the buttons labeled One Time to enter a Bill To and Ship To Address for the Cash Sale and use it on the Scratch Pad.

Enter the address in the Address text boxes. These are free form fields and you may enter the address in any format as long as the City and Postal Code are included. Select the operating entity and default inventory group from the corresponding drop-down boxes. If applicable, select the state, province, or region from the drop-down box labeled State/Region. Enter the City and Postal Code in the corresponding text boxes. Select the country from the Country drop-down box.

After all required fields have been completed, click the OK button to add the Address to the Scratch Pad.

Figure 3

The address entered is populated on the Scratch Pad. Be sure to complete this process for both the Bill To and the Ship To.

Figure 4

Select the appropriate credit terms from the drop-down list labeled Credit Terms. Select cc at Order if a credit card number is required before processing the order. Select cc Before Shipment if the order can be processed but a credit card is needed before the material can ship. Select Cash at Order if a cash payment or is required before processing the order. Select Cash Before Shipment if the order can be processed but a cash payment is needed before the material can ship.

Scratch Pads, Quotes. or Material Requests can now be created using the Cash Sales customer. Confirmed Material Requests for Cash Sales or Credit Card purchases must be released by the Finance Department from the MR Release page. For more information on the MR Release process, see the MR Release help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/MRRelease/MRRelease.htm.
