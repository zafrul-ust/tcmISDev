New Customer Contact

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The New Customer Contact process is used to add new customer contacts and contact information to tcmIS for Distribution Hubs. You may begin the New Customer Contact process from the Scratch Pad by clicking the labeled Contact.

If a new customer contact needs to be added, click the New Contact button.

Figure 1

The New Customer Contact page is displayed. All fields marked with an asterisk are required fields and must be completed.

Enter the customer contact's First Name and Last Name in the corresponding text boxes.

Create a nickname for this customer contact and enter it in the Nick Name text box.

Select the customer contact's primary role from the Job Function drop-down list.

Enter the customer contact's telephone number in the Phone text box and if known, enter the customer contact's mobile phone number is the text box labeled Mobile.

Enter the customer contact's email address in the Email text box.

Enter the customer contact's fax number in the Fax text box, if known.

Select any of the check boxes labeled Purchasing, Accounts Payable, Receiving, Quality Assurance, Management, or Other if the customer contact also performs any of these roles.

Figure 2

After all required fields have been completed, add the new customer contact by clicking the OK button.

To update existing customer information, you should access the Customer Search page from the Distribution menu. For more information on viewing and updating existing customer information, see the Customer Search help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/CustomerSearch.htm.
