MV Items

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

Some items in tcmIS not always the same size each time delivered from the supplier. For example, a 55 gal drum may be filled to 50 gal sometimes and 53 gallons at other times. Some rolls may have approximate lengths but vary by plus or minus 10% each time delivered. These types of items are known in tcmIS as MV Items, or Materials of Variable Size.

MV Items are displayed differently than regular materials when entered in the tcmIS Distribution screens. After adding an MV Item to a Scratch Pad, Quote, or MAterial Request, you will notice that the description of the material includes the words "size varies" and a nominal quantity.

In the Order Qty field you should enter the quantity of the item desired. For example, the number of rolls or drums required. The Variable Size area displays the order quantity multiplied by the approximate size of the item in the Nominal Qty field. The unit of measure in which the material is sold is displayed in the UOS column. Enter the price per unit in the UOSP column. The extended price is calculated by multiplying the Nominal Qty times the UOSP.

When the material is received the actual size of the material is recorded into tcmIS and invoiced to the customer.
