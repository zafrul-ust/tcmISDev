New Material

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The New Material process is used when a new customer needs a quote or material request and the Catalog Item needed does not exist in the tcmIS Distribution Catalog. When adding a line to a Scratch Pad, Quote, or Material request and the desired product is not located, click the Search Global Item link above the data grid to search all items in tcmIS which may not be in the Distribution Catalog.

Figure 1

Enter the Item ID, manufacturer's part number, or description in the Search box and click Search. If the desired item is located highlight the line by clicking anywhere on the line and then click the Add Item link.

Figure 2

If the material is not located in the Global Catalog, click the New Material link.

Complete the fields as described below. Required fields are marked with a *.

If known, enter the shipping weight. Select the unit of measure for the shipping weight from the Unit drop-down list.

Enter the name of the product in the Material Description field. Include all necessary information to procure the product including color, type, etc.

Enter any information regarding the grade of the material in the Grade text box.

If the material has a specific size not described in the previous fields, enter it in the Dimension text box. For example, a roll of film may have a length, width, and thickness.

Figure 3

Select the manufacturer by clicking the to the right of the Manufacturer text box.

Enter the name of the manufacturer of the product in the Search box and click the Search button. Select the manufacturer by clicking anywhere on the desired line and clicking the Return Selected Manufacturer link.

Figure 4

The Manufacturer field is now populated on the New Catalog Item screen. If the manufacturer was not found in the list, simply type the name of the manufacturer in the text box.

If you have a specific contact at the manufacturer you have already spoken with, please enter the name and phone number in the Manufacturer's Contact text box.

Enter any special notes regarding the product or the manufacturer in the text box labeled Manufacturer's Notes.

If the manufacturer has a web site, please enter the URL in the Manufacturer's URL box.

Enter the Manufacturer's Trade name as it appears on the MSDS in the corresponding text field.

If known, enter the Manufacturer's Part Number.

Enter the number of units in the # Per Part box. Enter the package size in the Size box and select the unit of measure from the drops-down list labeled Unit. The number of units should be the smallest individual package that can be purchased. For drums, pails, rolls, cans, etc, this is typically 1.

Select the package style by clicking the to the right of the Package Style text box.

Figure 5

Enter the desired package style in the Search box and click the Search button. Select the desired package style by clicking anywhere on the line and clicking the Return Selected Package Style link above the data grid.

Figure 6

The selected package style is populated on the New Catalog Item page.

If the material has only 1 component, click the Submit link at the top left of the New Catalog Item page.

Figure 7

If the material is a commercial item and certifications are not available from the manufacturer, check the box labeled Manufacturer Cert not available.

Figure 8

If the product has more than one component (or a kit) right-click on the Component tab and select Add Component.

Figure 9

A new tab is created for the additional component of the kit. Complete the required fields for each component of the kit. You may add as many components as needed. After all components which comprise the kit are added, click the Submit link at the top left of the New Catalog Item page.

Figure 10

If the desired material is located in the Global Catalog search, but the packaging size or style is not what is needed, highlight the line by clicking anywhere on the line. Click the New Packaging link above the data grid.

Figure 11

Enter the Size and Packing information as described above. After all required information has been entered, click the Submit link in the top right of the New Catalog Item page.

Click the Cancel link in the top right of the New Catalog Item page to cancel the request without submitting.

Figure 12

After clicking Submit, the new material will be added to the Scratch Pad, Quote, or MR. If a New Material or New Packaging request was submitted, the item will be a temporary item and will be validated by the Catalog Team.
