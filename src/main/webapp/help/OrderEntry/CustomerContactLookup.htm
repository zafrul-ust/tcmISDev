Customer Contact Lookup

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Customer Contact Lookup function is used to locate current distribution customer contact information from a Scratch Pad. A customer contact should be selected on a Scratch Pad to begin the Quoting or Material Request entry process.

You may begin the Customer Contact Lookup process from the Scratch Pad by clicking the labeled Contact. The Customer must be selected first. For more information on the Customer Lookup process, see https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/CustomerLookup.htm.

Figure 1

By default, all customer contact information for the customer selected on the Scratch Pad is displayed.

If you hover above some data cells, a tool tip will pop up and display the contents of the data cell. Typically tool tips will appear on columns where the data in the cell contains over 30 characters and the default column width does not display the entire contents of the cell. Use this feature to display the contents of cells without widening the column.

Figure 2

You may locate a customer contact using the contact name. Select contains from the Contact drop-down list and enter a portion of the contact name in the Contact text box. Click Search.

Figure 3

You may locate a customer contact using the contact telephone number. Select contains from the Telephone drop-down list and enter a portion of the telephone number in the Telephone text box. Click Search.

Figure 4

You may locate a customer contact using the contact email address. Select contains from the Email drop-down list and enter a portion of the email address in the Email text box. Click Search.

Figure 5

Check the box labeled Show Inactive to display only inactive customer contacts. Click Search.

Figure 6

To select a customer contact and add it to the Scratch Pad simply click the desired customer contact and then click the Return Selected Contact link above the data grid. The customer contact will be populated on the Scratch Pad.

Figure 7

Click the Create Excel Button to export the results in the data grid to Excel format.

Click the New Contact button to create a new customer contact. For more information on adding a new customer contact, see the New Customer Contact help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/OrderEntry/NewCustomerContact.htm.
