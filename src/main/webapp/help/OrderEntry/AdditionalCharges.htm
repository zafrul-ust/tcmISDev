Additional Charges

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Additional Charges process is used to add header and/or line charges to a Scratch Pad, Quote, or Material Request.

Click the Hdr Charges button in the header to add additional charges to the Scratch Pad, Quote, or MR header. These charges apply to the entire order as opposed to additional line charges which apply only to specific lines on the order. Header additional charges can be used to create miscellaneous credits and debits witch are not associated with material lines.

Figure 1

Click the Add Line link to add a new charge. Select the type of charge from the Charge Description drop-down list.

Figure 2

If the desired charge is not found in the Charge Description drop-down list, you may type a description of the charge in the Charge Description text box.

Enter the price in the Price column. Select Charged Only Once from the Charge drop down list if the fee is charged only once regardless of the number of deliveries. Select Charged once per Delivery if the fee is charged on every delivery. Click the Update link to add the fee to the Scratch Pad, Quote, or MR.

Figure 3

You may add additional fees by clicking in the Add Line link and repeating the process. Remove additional charges by clicking anywhere on the line and clicking the Remove Line link.

When finished adding header additional charges, press the ESC button or close the Header Additional Charges window.

Figure 4

You may add additional charges to individual material lines by right-clicking anywhere on the line and selecting Additional Charges. These charges apply to the selected material line only as opposed to header additional charges which apply the entire order.

Figure 5

Click the Add Line link to add a new charge. Select the type of charge from the Charge Description drop-down list. Enter the price in the Price column. Select Charged Only Once from the Charge drop down list if the fee is charged only once regardless of the number of deliveries. Select Charged once per Delivery if the fee is charged on every delivery. Select Charged per Unit Delivered if the fee is charged for every unit shipped. Click the Update link to add the fee to the Scratch Pad, Quote, or MR.

Figure 6

You may add additional fees to the line by clicking the Add Line link and repeating the process. Remove additional charges by clicking anywhere on the line and clicking the Remove Line link.

When finished adding additional charges to the selected line, press the ESC button or close the Additional Charges window.

Figure 7

The total additional charges, including both header charges and line charges, is shown in the header. The total line charges for each line on the Scratch Pad, Quote, or Material Request is displayed in the Addl Charges column.
