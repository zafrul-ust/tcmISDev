Credit Control

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Credit Control process manages orders for customers who are have exceeded their credit limit or credit terms.

The Credit Department can manage a customer's credit limit and status from the Customer Details page. For more information on maintaining customer details, see the Customer Search help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/CustomerSearch/CustomerSearch.htm.

Figure 1

If a customer's Credit Status is changed to Stop, Material Requests can not be shipped. All open MRs will be cancelled and the buyer will be notified to cancel any open Purchase Orders.

Figure 2

If a customer's Payment Terms are Credit Card, Check with Order, Cash at Pickup, Cash at Order, Cash Before Shipment, or COD, all Material Requests for that customer must be released from the MR Release page. For more details on the MR Release process, see the MR Release help page: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/MRRelease/MRRelease.htm.

When a customer that has exceeded their credit limit or credit terms is added to a Scratch Pad, Quote, or Material Request, an Over Credit Limit warning is displayed. Click the Review button to view more information regarding the customer's credit status.

Figure 3

Details regarding the customer's account are displayed. Unapplied Cash, Open Invoices, and Open Orders are displayed.
