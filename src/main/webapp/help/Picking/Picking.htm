Picking

+ Larger Font | - Smaller Font

A pick is an instruction in tcmIS to obtain material from inventory to satisfy a customer's Material Request. Picked materials may be either routinely stocked MM items or OOR items ordered specially for the customer. An OOR is in Haas TCM inventory only so long as necessary to complete quality reviews and labeling.

Watch the following movie to learn more about the Picking process: https://www.tcmis.com/tcmIS/help/Picking/PickingMovie.htm.

Picking is completed in tcmIS on the Picking page. This page can be accessed from the Supply Chain Menu. Select Stock Room Operations. Under Delivery, choose Picking.

Select the Hub for which picking will be performed from the Hub drop-down box. Click Search to display all Material Requests which are ready to be picked.

The results may be sorted by Facility/Work Area, Material Request ID, Part Number, or date needed. Select the desired sort option from the Sort drop-down list. The default is to sort by Facility/Work Area.

Figure 2

To display picks for a specific facility, select the desired facility from the Facility drop-down box and click Search.

To locate a pick for a specific Material Request, enter the MR number in the MR text box and click Search.

Materials are retrieved by warehouse personnel from Haas TCM inventory using the pick list. TcmIS selects materials for shipping on the basis of lot status and age. Only materials with lot status Available or Pending Recert may be picked. If a given material has multiple Receipt IDs, tcmIS will request the item with the oldest original receipt date using a First In/First Out (FIFO) algorithm. This will occur even if the shelf life of a material has been extended through the recertification process making its expiration date later than other items in inventory. The pick list shows the receipt ID of the item which is to be picked as well as other available receipt IDs. If a receipt ID can not be located and/or is picked outside of the FIFO algorithm, the pick list is annotated to provide the quantity of the selected receipt ID together with an explanation of why it was picked.

It is important to note that printing the pick list decrements the Haas TCM inventory as reflected on the Logistics page.

Picking QC is the process of independently checking materials picked for delivery before final packaging and shipment to the customer. The completed pick list is compared against material staged for delivery to confirm that the correct receipt ID and quantity has been picked and that the materials are properly labeled. To complete the delivery, proceed to the Picking QC page.
