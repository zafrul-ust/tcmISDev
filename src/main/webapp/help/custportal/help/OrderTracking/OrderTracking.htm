Order Tracking

The Order Tracking page allows you to determine the status of an Order (aka, Material Request or MR) in tcmIS. The Order Tracking page also provides a menu item to send a question to HGI about an order.

The Order Tracking page is accessed from the Order Tracking menu by clicking on the Order Tracking menu item. The Order Tracking page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

The Order Tracking page allows you to tailor your search for requested materials and view their status. You must choose the customer for your search from the Customer list. You may narrow your search to items shipped to a specific location by selecting it from the Ship To list, or leave that at the default of All locations.

  • Using the Status selection box, you may choose to search for All orders, leave the default of Open orders, or request only Complete[d] orders.
  • To locate orders from a specific time period, use the two fields labeled Order Date: From...To. Each time you place your mouse cursor in one of these fields, the Date Selector form is displayed. You may choose a date for From, To, or both.
    • Choose each date by clicking the date on the calendar. The dates selected will appear on the Order Tracking page.
    • For a specific date: enter the same date in both fields.
    • For a date range: enter the earliest date in the left field and the latest date in the right field.
  • To locate orders based on the promised ship date, use the two fields labeled Promised Ship Date: From...To. Each time you place your mouse cursor in one of these fields, the Date Selector form is displayed. You may choose a date for From, To, or both.
    • Choose each date by clicking the date on the calendar. The dates selected will appear on the Order Tracking page.
    • For a specific date: enter the same date in both fields.
    • For a date range: enter the earliest date in the left field and the latest date in the right field.
  • You may provide something to at least partially identify the material you want using the Search boxes by providing partial or full text for the MR (material Request number), the Catalog Item [number], the Customer PO (which is the default selection), or the Customer Part Number. See Search functions for more information.

Click the Search button to begin the search. tcmIS will display the records it finds in a data grid in the lower panel of the Order Tracking page.

The data grid contains a single record on each row/line with values for the record in the named columns. This is a rather wide grid so it will be necessary to scroll to the right to see information that does not fit on the initial display. Use the scroll bar near the bottom of the panel to move your 'view' of the data left and right as needed.

Right click in any column of any record and two things will happen.

  • The record row will be 'selected': this is visually indicated by the background of the row changing to a light purple color.
  • A small 'context menu' will be displayed: this menu allows you to choose one of three actions.
  • A left click on a record will also select and highlight it but will not display the context menu.
Figure 5

On the context menu, selecting the Order Status menu item will open a child window and display an Order Status form. There will be a record for every line in the order. (If there is only a single line on the order, the Order Status form and the Order Line Status form (see below) will display the same information.)

In addition to reading the information on this form, there are four actions you can take.

  • Click on the name of the CSR, displayed as a link, in the upper left corner, to display a small window that will appear superimposed on the form showing information about the CSR.
  • Some columns may be too narrow to display all their data: hover your mouse on any of these columns and the data will be displayed in a small box known as a 'tool tip'.
  • Right click on any data in the left half of the form for a context menu item to Print the record.
  • Right click on data at the right end of a record for a context menu to
    • Display Certs will open another child window to display any scanned OEM Certificates for the shipment (this menu item is displayed only if OEM Certificates were scanned in during the receiving process);
    • Display MSDS will open another child window to display a stored MSDS for the material (if this item is not active, the Request MSDS item will be available);
    • Request MSDS will send an email to the CSR and the HGI Catalog Team requesting the MSDS be entered into the Portal (this item is not available if Display MSDS is active);
    • Display Packing List will open another child window to display the packing list for the shipment (this menu item is displayed only if the MR is ship confirmed);
    • Display Invoice will open another child window to display the invoice for the shipment (this menu item is displayed only if your user account has permissions to view invoices).

Figure 7

On the context menu, selecting the Contact Us Concerning This Order menu item will open a child window and display a Contact form. Complete this form:

  • Select your topic from the Subject list: Request Delivery Confirmation, Expedite Order, or Other.
  • Enter details about your situation, if applicable, in the large text box.
  • Click the Submit button to send the inquiry about the record to HGI.
