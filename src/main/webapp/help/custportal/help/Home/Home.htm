HGI Customer Portal

The HGI Customer Portal home page is located at https://apps.tcmis.com/tcmIS/customer/home.do. It is also easily accessible from the Haas Group International home page by clicking the Distribution Customer Portal link in the upper-left corner.

Enter your Logon ID and Password in their respective boxes in the lower left corner. Choose your preferred language from the Language drop-down list. Click the Login button to enter the Portal.

If you have forgotten your password you may click on the Forgot Password link for help.
