Navigation

The HGI Customer Portal begins with a simple menu horizontally across the upper portion of the page. As your move your mouse pointer across the menu items drop-down selections are displayed that provide access to all (and only) the pages for which you have permission.

Figure 1

Each time you use the menu system to navigate to a particular page, a tabbed window is opened to display the selected page. You may switch from one open page (tab) to another by clicking on the tab of the page you want to view. Each page will maintain the data as you last left it.

Clicking on the small x on the right end of any tab will cause that tabbed window to close. Or, you may right click on the tab and select the Close Tab item on the menu. (See below.)

Figure 2: One tab open
Figure 3: Two tabs open

For convenience you may control which tabs open automatically whenever you start the HGI Customer Portal. Right click on the tab of the currently active tab and you will get a context menu of three choices:

  • Close Tab;
  • Add Tab to Startup;
  • Remove Tab from Startup.

Select whichever of these three fits your requirement.
