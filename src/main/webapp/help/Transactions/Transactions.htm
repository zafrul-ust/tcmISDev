The Transactions page is used to view history of all receipts, issues, or lot status changes in tcmIS. The Transactions page can be accessed from the Supply Chain menu by selecting Stock Room Inventory. Select Inventory then Transactions. The Transactions page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select the Hub and/or Inventory Group for which transactions are to be viewed. To see all recent transactions for a hub and/or Inventory Group, enter the number of days back for which you are interested in. Click Search.

Figure 2

To see all transactions for a specific Item ID, enter the Item ID in the corresponding text box and click Search.

Figure 3

To see all transactions for a specific Receipt ID, enter the Receipt ID in the corresponding text box and click Search.

Figure 4

To see all transactions entered on a specific date, enter the date in the text box labeled On Date by clicking in the text box and selecting the date from the calendar. Click Search.

Figure 5

To see all transactions for a specific Material Request, enter the MR Number (do not include the line item) in the text box labeled MR and click Search.

Figure 6

To see all transactions for a specific Manufacturer's Lot Number or Batch Number, enter the Lot Number in the text box labeled Mfg Lot and click Search.

Figure 7

To see all transactions for a specific Tracking Number, enter the Tracking Number in the corresponding text box and click Search.

Figure 8

To see all transactions for a specific Purchase Order, enter the PO Number in the text box labeled PO and click Search.

Figure 9

You may limit the transactions to only receipts into inventory by selecting Receipts as the Trans Type.

Figure 10

You may limit the transactions to only issues from inventory by selecting Issues as the Trans Type.

Figure 11

You may also limit the transactions to only transfers from other warehouses by selecting Transfers as the Trans Type.

Figure 12

Results can be sorted by Date of Receipt, Expire Date, Item ID, PO, Receipt ID, or Transaction Date.

Figure 13

The data on the Transactions page can also be sorted by certain columns by clicking the column name. The results will be sorted by the selected column in ascending order. Click the column name again to sort in descending order.

Figure 14

On the Transactions page, the column labeled Type contains an abbreviation that will indicate what type of transaction was made. The Transaction Types are listed below.

IA - Inventory Adjustment. This is typically a receipt of customer owned or zero cost material into inventory in tcmIS.

OV - Original Voucher. This is a receipt of material against a tcmIS Purchase Order.

LC - Lot Status Change. This is a change of Lot Status, typically from Incoming to Available as a result Receipt QC step.

RI - Regular Issue. This is a delivery from inventory to a customer.

IT - Inventory Transfer. This is an inventory transfer from another hub.

IC -Item Conversion. This is the tapping of a drum or container for dispensing.

If you hover above some data cells, a tool tip will pop up and display the contents of the data cell. Typically tool tips will appear on columns where the data in the cell contains over 30 characters and the default column width does not display the entire contents of the cell. Use this feature to display the contents of cells without widening the column.
