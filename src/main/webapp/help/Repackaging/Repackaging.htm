Repackaging

+ Larger Font | + Smaller Font

The Repackaging page facilitates the onsite repackaging and relabeling of material by Haas TCM Representatives in tcmIS. The Repackaging page can be accessed from the Stock Room Operations menu by selecting Receiving. Then select Repackaging. The Repackaging page is launched in a new tab.

Figure 1

Select the hub and inventory group for which repackaging is to be performed from the corresponding drop-down boxes. Click Search to see all open repackaging requests.

Figure 2

You may enter an Item ID in the Search box and click Search to display repackaging requests for the item.

Figure 3

You may enter an item description in the Search box and click Search to display repackaging requests for the material.

Figure 4

The quantity requested to be repackaged and the quantity available are displayed. Enter the quantity that has been repackaged in the QTY Repackaged column. Select the bin where the repackaged material will be stored from the Bin drop-down list. Click the + to the left of the bin to select from the list of active bins in the warehouse. Click Process to create the repackaged receipts. Labels for the repackaged materials will be generated. Check the box labeled Skip Kit/Case Qty Labels to print only container labels.

Figure 5

If there is not enough material available to fulfill the repackaging request, click the link in the Item column to open or tap a new container. All available inventory to tap is displayed. Select the Receipt ID to tap by checking the box in the OK column. Select the bin location where the tapped container will reside from the Bin drop-down box. Click the + to the left of the bin to select from the list of active bins in the warehouse. Click Tap to open the container and make it available for fulfillment of repackaging requests.

Figure 6

A label for the tapped container is displayed for printing.

Figure 7

On the Repackaging page, click Close to close empty containers. Click Close Tap.

Figure 8

On the Repackaging page, click Force Repackaging to generate a repackaging request. Enter the Item ID or a description of the material to be repackaged and click Search. Locate the material and packaging and enter the quantity needed in the Qty column. Click Force Order. The repackaging request in now visible on the Repackaging page.

Figure 9

On the Repackaging page, click Undo Tap to reverse the opening or tapping of a container. Click the line to close and click Reverse Selected Tap.
